# Companions

## Brannok-Side

### Rook / Central Bird

Soot-black crow. Cannot speak words. Clever, rude, loyal on his own terms, emotionally important, loves bread and petty authority. Do not kill casually. Does not get independent combat turns unless the player directs or the scene specifically supports it.

### Nera Cale

Carter's Rest innkeeper. Practical, sharp, protective, emotionally guarded. Feeds people like she is threatening them. Stays with Carter's Rest; does not leave the inn for the road.

### Mirelle Thorn

Legal/evidence/cipher expert. Brave through fear. Dry, socially useful, not a frontline fighter. Traveling because Last Lantern and Iron Road evidence requires someone who can read route marks, writs, plates, and courier shorthand.

### Corren Dask

Young Ward guard, Certified Survivor, back-table defender, witness to the Princess Carry Incident. Eager, useful, must not become reckless proof-of-worth bait.

## Selene-Side

### Ves

Cat-sized pseudodragon familiar. Not a cat. Cannot speak words. Proud, possessive, dramatic, loyal to Selene, emotionally important.

### Marcella Viretti

Selene's romantic partner. Elegant social blade, sharp, stylish, proud, protective, vulnerability hidden in precision.

### Kara

Life Cleric. Protector, faith, mercy with backbone, frontline shield.

### Eya Harisa

Knowledge Cleric. Survivor care, memory, gentle truth, emotional stabilizer.

### Jasna

Mastermind Rogue / Knowledge Cleric. Legal/evidence knife, records, public truth, official consequence.

### Zula Ozu

Artificer / Divination Wizard. Wardcraft, tattoos, practical magic, counter-magic, dangerous curiosity with a conscience.

### Nessa Vale / Needle

Tabaxi rogue. Barefoot, dark/mysterious, architecture menace, deflects care. Named as a Daughter; her name is protected.

### Mara

Battle Master Fighter. Blunt wall, "the door," protective and not soft about it.

## Support-Wing Rule

In large scenes, do not run everyone as full initiative. Assign support objectives such as evacuate children, hold wagons, stabilize wards, protect evidence, block exits, or counter a secondary threat.
