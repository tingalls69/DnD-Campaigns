## The Paper Knife At Greybridge

Time: morning through late morning in Greybridge, immediately after the Hearthline rescue and the party's night at the wayhouse.

Starting location: Greybridge wayhouse / magistrate-controlled shelter.

Ending location: same location, secured after the failed false custody transfer and yard attack.

Final stopping point: the party completed a short rest inside the Greybridge wayhouse. Prisoners are separated and bound. Pella Morn is safe for now near the hearth with Mara and Rook nearby. Mirelle is cataloguing evidence. Zula has bound the road-arch tokens. Selene has checked Ves's wound. Brannok has confirmed prisoner binds and searches. Next likely action is planning the approach to Old Bellweather House / South Lantern Relay.

Major events:

- A false official calling himself Deputy Custody Officer Halven Rusk arrived with wagons, escort guards, and transfer paperwork claiming authority to relocate Hearthline witnesses.
- Mirelle identified that the seal was real but the order was wrong: the destination line pointed to South Lantern Relief Annex, a decommissioned or false destination.
- Selene publicly challenged Rusk. Brannok backed her in direct Brannok fashion.
- Rusk revealed the transfer was specifically aimed at Pella Morn, a young Hearthline witness with a burn scar on her wrist.
- A coordinated attack began: rooftop knifemen, hook-blade wagon attackers, and a masked coordinator with a matching road-arch token.
- Selene blasted the horse attacker, later dropped Rusk with Eldritch Blast, and wounded the masked coordinator.
- Ves stung both rooftop knifemen unconscious, preventing them from reaching the civilians from behind.
- Brannok grappled Rusk before he could cleanly activate his token, stopped him from fleeing with Sentinel, flattened multiple escort guards, moved into the yard, and killed/downed the horse striker.
- Zula bound and destroyed/suppressed the indoor road-arch token, then helped bind the outside token with Selene's assistance.
- Mara held the civilian line, prevented access to Pella, took 8 damage, and dropped Escort Guard 3.
- Marcella gave Bardic Inspiration to Selene, used Dissonant Whispers on Rusk, and helped manage morale, panic, and tempo.
- Corren fought hook-blade attackers in the yard, took 9 damage, and helped stabilize the masked coordinator.
- Brindle and Greybridge guards secured the wagons and prisoners.
- Brannok directed prisoner roundup procedures: bind, search, recheck binds, separate Rusk and the masked coordinator, gag them, and check the mask for magic.
- Brannok personally searched Rusk and the prisoners thoroughly, finding missed hidden weapons and evidence.
- Pella revealed a hidden servant-road entrance to Bellweather House: under the rain steps, behind the old ash trough, accessible when water is running; the stones move correctly only when water runs.
- Pella also revealed she has heard the south bell once before, in her sleep.
- A recovered note said: "Take the Morn girl alive. Paper first. Knife second. Bellweather if exposed. Do not let her hear the south bell twice."
- The group used humor, Rook's terrible dancing, and Ves's proud recovery to help Pella regain some steadiness after the attack.
