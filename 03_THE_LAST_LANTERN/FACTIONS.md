# Factions

## The Hearthline

Refugee and witness convoy. Soup, wagons, tired guards, children, livestock, broken wheels, evidence satchels, scared people, and heroes who need to sleep but will not.

## The Iron Road

Not just a road. A web of trade routes, ward-lines, old survey stations, relief houses, prisons, inns, wayhouses, markets, and communities that live or die by whether travel is safe.

## Road-Ward Infrastructure

Practical fantasy infrastructure. Wards protect roads from monsters, raiders, beasts, ambushes, and route collapse. Damaged or falsified wards make roads unsafe.

## Thresh Network

Secular/institutional logistics network using old road authority, current corruption, and fear. Their evil is not worshiping a concept. Their evil is making spreadsheets out of suffering and calling it civilization.

## Vellford / Black Rose / Domestic Benevolence Remnants

False-name and witness-erasure structures broken open by Selene and the Daughters. Remnants may still flee, hide, sanitize, or destroy evidence, but the Vellford finale is complete.

## Carter's Rest

Emotional anchor. Refuge, meals, arguments, Rook's crimes, Nera's threats, exhausted laughter, and the place the campaign remembers why roads matter.
