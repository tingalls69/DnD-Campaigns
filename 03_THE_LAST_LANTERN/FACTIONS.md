# Factions

## The Hearthline

Refugee and witness convoy. Soup, wagons, tired guards, children, livestock, broken wheels, evidence satchels, scared people, and heroes who need to sleep but will not.

The Hearthline survived the South Lantern Waystation attack and reached Greybridge. It is now vulnerable to custody theft by paperwork.

## The Iron Road

Not just a road. A web of trade routes, ward-lines, old survey stations, relief houses, prisons, inns, wayhouses, markets, and communities that live or die by whether travel is safe.

## Road-Ward Infrastructure

Practical fantasy infrastructure. Wards protect roads from monsters, raiders, beasts, ambushes, and route collapse. Damaged or falsified wards make roads unsafe.

## Thresh Network

Secular/institutional logistics network using old road authority, current corruption, false safety designations, and fear.

Their evil is not worshiping a concept. Their evil is making spreadsheets out of suffering and calling it civilization.

Current methods:

- false ward-iron
- route closures
- custody transfers
- witness seizure
- forged/old Road Works orders
- compromised local guards
- safe-looking routes designed to fail
- direct violence when people are exposed
- paperwork when people are protected

## South Lantern Field Chain

Operational field layer of the Thresh Network.

Known elements:

- South Lantern / Bellmother / Unlit Hand
- gray-scarf signalers
- brown-hooded couriers
- green-cuff auxiliaries
- mill sluice routes
- hidden ice-cellar escape cuts
- Old Bellweather House / South Lantern Relay

## Vellford / Black Rose / Domestic Benevolence Remnants

False-name and witness-erasure structures broken open by Selene and the Daughters. Remnants may still flee, hide, sanitize, or destroy evidence, but the Vellford finale is complete.

This should not drag the campaign back into true-name/name-magic as the main arc.

## Carter's Rest

Emotional anchor. Refuge, meals, arguments, Rook's crimes, Nera's threats, exhausted laughter, and the place the campaign remembers why roads matter.

Nera has been warned that Carter's Rest / Stonewake survivors may be threatened directly or indirectly.
