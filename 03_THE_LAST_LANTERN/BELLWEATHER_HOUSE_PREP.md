# Bellweather House Prep - AI Run Card

Load when planning/running Old Bellweather House / South Lantern Relay.

## Launch

- Start point: Greybridge wayhouse after short rest.
- Stakes: captured prisoners + evidence + Pella route + south bell risk.
- Player choices: interrogate, examine evidence, ask Pella, secure Greybridge, choose route/timing/team.

## Routes

| Route | Use | Risks |
|---|---|---|
| Main Road | direct, visible, official pressure possible | watched, roadblock, false ward, cleanup ambush |
| Hidden Servant-Road | Pella's route: under rain steps, behind old ash trough, works when water runs | trapped/flooded/watched; south-bell tie; Pella involvement risk |
| Official Bluff | use evidence/prisoners/Greybridge authority to approach openly | parley/trap; evidence targeted; witnesses left behind endangered |

## Pella Rule

Pella is a person, not a key.

- May provide: route landmarks, sensory memories, south-bell reaction, small clue.
- Must not be forced to travel, blamed, used as lore dispenser, or exposed to second bell without clear player-facing risk.
- Load `protected_characters/Pella_Morn.md` before portrayal.

## South Bell Clock

| Clock | Trigger | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 0/4 | start | Pella heard the south bell once in sleep |
| 1/4 | relay hum/token pulse/mask feedback | scar pressure; Zula/Selene can notice |
| 2/4 | nearby bell-linked magic | partial note; freeze/dissociation/memory flash |
| 3/4 | intentional relay targeting | scar reacts; tokens/beads may answer |
| 4/4 | second full hearing | earned consequence: mark, command attempt, location ping, memory unlock, or relay activation; not instant death |

Reduce/pause: Zula wards, Selene threshold magic/Dispel Magic, distance Pella, muffle/disable bell, understand mask/tokens first, player protection plan.

## Required Bellweather Content

- At least one rescuable person alive now.
- Field ledger or route evidence.
- South Lantern sign/contact.
- Tactical choice where not everything can be handled by one PC.
- Hazard where breaking wrong thing destroys evidence or harms captives.

## Spotlight Lanes

- Brannok: hold route, stop runner, choose what he personally protects.
- Selene: command Ves, challenge control, threshold/patron pressure.
- Marcella: social tempo, Cutting Words, counter-pressure.
- Mara: door/chokepoint/bell access.
- Needle: scout, trap, hidden hostage/evidence.
- Zula: wards, tokens, mask, relay.
- Mirelle: records, seals, contradictions.
- Corren: grapple, restrain, drag civilians.
- Rook: morale/child grounding.
- Ves: scouting, sting, threshold warning.

## South Lantern Contact Options

1. Bell voice.
2. Mask repeats/resists a phrase.
3. Cane/unlit lantern sign.
4. Parley: trade hostages/records for Pella/evidence/withdrawal.
5. Abandonment: cuts off failed agents.

## Avoid

- Document-only dungeon.
- Unsignaled south-bell gotcha.
- Forced Pella danger.
- Companions solving while PCs watch.
- Paperwork replacing endangered people.
