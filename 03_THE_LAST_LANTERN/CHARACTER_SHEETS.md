### Feat Mechanics

#### Crusher

- +1 STR already included in final scores.
- Once per turn, when Brannok hits a creature with bludgeoning damage, he can move it 5 feet to an unoccupied space if it is no more than one size larger than him.
- When Brannok scores a critical hit that deals bludgeoning damage, attacks against that creature have advantage until the start of Brannok's next turn.

#### Sentinel

- When Brannok hits a creature with an opportunity attack, that creature's speed becomes 0 for the rest of the turn.
- Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from Brannok even if they Disengage before leaving his reach.
- When a creature within 5 feet of Brannok attacks a target other than him, and that target does not also have Sentinel, Brannok can use his reaction to make a melee weapon attack against the attacker.

- Athletics has Expertise.

### Patron Features

#### Fey Presence

As an action, Selene can force creatures in a 10-foot cube from her to make a Wisdom save. On a failed save, they are charmed or frightened by Selene until the end of her next turn. Recharges on short or long rest.

#### Misty Escape

When Selene takes damage, she can use her reaction to turn invisible and teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space she can see. She remains invisible until the start of her next turn or until she attacks/casts. Recharges on short or long rest.

#### Beguiling Defenses

Selene is immune to being charmed. When another creature attempts to charm her, she can use her reaction to attempt to charm that creature back if it fails a Wisdom save.

## Homebrew Mechanics — Zula Ozu, Wardwright Tattooist Artificer

Use Artificer as the base class. Zula's subclass is a campaign homebrew focused on tattoos, wards, countermarks, and magical engineering.

### Level 3: Countermark Ink

After a long rest, Zula can mark a number of willing creatures equal to her Intelligence modifier. A marked creature can expend the mark once before Zula's next long rest to add 1d4 to one saving throw or reduce incoming damage by 1d4 + Zula's Intelligence modifier.

A creature can only carry one of Zula's active countermarks at a time.

### Level 3: Wardwright Spells

Always prepared if Zula has spell slots of that level:

- Shield
- Protection from Evil and Good

### Level 5: Ward-Sting Reaction

When a creature bearing Zula's countermark is hit by an attack within 60 feet of her, Zula can use her reaction to flare the mark. The marked creature reduces the damage by 1d6 + Zula's Intelligence modifier, and the attacker takes force damage equal to Zula's Intelligence modifier.

Uses per long rest: proficiency bonus.

### Level 5: Wardwright Spells

Always prepared:

- Lesser Restoration
- Warding Bond

### Level 9: Living Ward Array

Zula can maintain one additional countermark beyond the normal limit. Also, when she casts Dispel Magic or uses an action to examine a glyph, ward, or magical mechanism, she has advantage on the related Arcana/Investigation check if tattooist's tools, calligrapher's supplies, or tinker's tools are usable.

Once per long rest, when she fails a check to suppress or understand a ward, she may reroll it.

### Level 9: Wardwright Spells

Always prepared:

- Counterspell
- Glyph of Warding
