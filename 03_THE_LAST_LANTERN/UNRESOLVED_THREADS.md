# Unresolved Threads

## The Hearthline Crossing

Status: Active.

Known: Refugees, witnesses, children, injured workers, rescued prisoners, and fragile evidence must move through unsafe roads.

Unknown: Which route is safest, who is already compromised, and what must be sacrificed if the party divides attention.

Status: Active villain thread.

Known: `O.T.` appears on the Stonewake forward handoff. Thresh is tied to old road authority, iron routing, and acceptable-loss logic.

Unknown: Current location, direct agents, political shields, and exact relationship to the Last Lantern hold.

Unknown: Whether Last Lantern is a place, waystation, courier hold, road shelter, cell, or operational command phrase.
