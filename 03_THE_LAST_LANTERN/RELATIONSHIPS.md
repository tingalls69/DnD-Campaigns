# Relationships

## Brannok And Selene

Initial contrast:

- Brannok: hammer, road, grit, direct action.
- Selene: name, witness, consequence, chosen family.

Likely first impressions:

- Brannok may see Selene as dramatic, dangerous, and maybe too comfortable with attention.
- Selene may see Brannok as stubborn, blunt, and hard to move.
- Both quickly recognize the other protects people when it costs something.

## Brannok And Core Allies

- Rook: beloved soot-black crow, cannot speak words, Central Bird, moral hazard around bread.
- Nera: trusted friend and refuge anchor; feeds people like a threat.
- Mirelle: legal/evidence ally, brave through fear, likely to travel with him.
- Voss: trusted practical Ward sergeant; survived the finale.
- Kaelis: survivor and testimony; no longer cargo.
- Pellan: respected craftsman and honest road-metal expert.
- Tovin: wardwright tied to practical fear and technical courage.
- Garrow: useful but dangerous; not forgiven.
- Corren Dask: young Ward guard, Certified Survivor, wants to help.

## Selene And The Daughters

- Marcella: romantic partner, social blade, elegant threat, emotionally central.
- Ves: proud nonverbal pseudodragon familiar; near-death deepened the bond.
- Kara: mercy with backbone, frontline protector.
- Eya: survivor care, memory, gentle truth.
- Jasna: records, law, evidence, truth made official.
- Zula: wardcraft, counter-magic, curious conscience.
- Nessa Vale / Needle: named as a Daughter; deflects care.
- Mara: bodyguard, blunt wall, "the door."

## Cross-Cast

- Nera will feed Selene and threaten her in the same breath.
- Mirelle and Marcella must remain distinct: Mirelle is ledger courage and social bridge; Marcella is courtly blade and intimate emotional precision.
- Voss respects results but watches legal risk.
- Pellan respects craft and may be wary of glamour.
- Tovin is terrified of both Selene's magic and Brannok's hammer in useful proportions.
- Rook and Ves are a comedy engine: rivalry, disdain, occasional cooperation, no speeches.
