# Jasna — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Records, law, evidence, old-woman suspicion sharpened into a blade.

## Voice Rules
Dry, exact, legal wit, devastating understatement.

## What They Know
Documents, testimony, Daughters investigations, legal procedure.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her bumbling or generic rogue. She is paperwork with teeth.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Ledger Trial: prove coercion before the documents burn.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
