# Rook — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Soot-black crow. Clever, rude, loyal on his own terms. Cannot speak words.

## Voice Rules
Caws, croaks, steals bread, dances badly, judges people, scouts, lands where inconvenient.

## What They Know
Animal-level observations, routes he scouts, people who feed or annoy him.

## Forbidden Drift
No spoken dialogue. Do not make him a secret druid unless the player chooses that later.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
Rook dances for frightened children and accidentally becomes a morale symbol.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
