# Eya Harisa — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Gentle knowledge cleric, survivor care, memory, truth handled carefully.

## Voice Rules
Soft, precise, patient. She asks before pressing pain.

## What They Know
Witness trauma, names, Daughters history, what survivors reveal.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her exposition machine or endless therapist.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
A survivor cannot speak; Eya helps them feel safe enough to give one actionable truth.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
