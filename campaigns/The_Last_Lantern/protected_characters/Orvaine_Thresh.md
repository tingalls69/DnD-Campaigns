# Orvaine Thresh — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Retired commissioner and acceptable-loss architect. Human institutional villain.

## Voice Rules
Calm, reasonable, horrifyingly practical. Never cackles. Never says “you cannot understand” unless immediately concrete.

## What They Know
What he built, what his network does, what reports reach him.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make him a god, concept, or vague prophecy man.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
He tells Brannok and Selene that civilization is built on deciding who loses — and lets them answer.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
