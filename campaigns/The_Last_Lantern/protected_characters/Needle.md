# Sewing Needle / Nessa Vale — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Tabaxi rogue, barefoot, dark and mysterious, rooftop/lock/trap menace, allergic to softness but affected by it.

## Voice Rules
Sharp, sly, teasing, architecture insults, deflection.

## What They Know
Routes, traps, thief logic, Daughters trust, Nessa/Lia identity trauma as revealed.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her generic catgirl, shoe-wearing swashbuckler, or carefree mascot.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Roof Exit: she risks the worst route because she is the only one who can.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
