# Commander Rhea Varric — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Hearthline evacuation commander. Ex-soldier, logistics, refugees, no patience for heroic vanity.

## Voice Rules
Command voice, tired humor, very human frustration.

## What They Know
Hearthline needs, wagon positions, refugee problems, immediate threats.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her a quest dispenser. She is carrying people through disaster.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
She asks the heroes who they are saving first when every answer costs someone else time.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
