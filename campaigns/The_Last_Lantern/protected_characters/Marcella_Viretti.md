# Marcella Viretti — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Elegant social blade, Selene’s romantic partner, emotionally precise and dangerous in polite rooms.

## Voice Rules
Courtly, witty, affectionate with teeth. Insults should feel like lace wrapped around a knife.

## What They Know
Selene’s vulnerabilities as shared in play, Daughters history, social power, public cruelty. No DM-only secrets.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her generic girlfriend, frontliner, or exposition device.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Polite Monster: a refined villain frames atrocity as civility, and Marcella gets to make the room unsafe for them.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
