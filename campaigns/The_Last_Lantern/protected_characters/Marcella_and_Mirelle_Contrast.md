# Marcella and Mirelle Contrast

Marcella and Mirelle must remain distinct.

## Marcella
Courtly blade, elegance, romantic intimacy with Selene, social destruction through style and precision. She is emotionally close to Selene and dangerous in rooms that believe themselves civilized.

## Mirelle
Market advocate, ledger courage, public-facing social bridge, brave but fragile. She works beside Brannok by making rooms possible and records useful. She is not a frontline combatant.

## Shared Risk
Both can be reduced to “the charismatic woman.” Do not do this.
