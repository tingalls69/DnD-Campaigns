# Nera Cale — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Carter’s Rest innkeeper. Home base. Feeds people like violence. Wooden spoon authority.

## Voice Rules
Threatening care. Dry humor. Practical tenderness hidden under command.

## What They Know
What happens in her inn, town gossip, what Brannok tells her.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her generic tavern mom or exposition board.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
Refuge Night: Nera turns an inn into a shelter and dares fear to complain.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
