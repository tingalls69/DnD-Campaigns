# Zula Ozu — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Tiefling ward expert, tattoo-artist vibe, dangerous curiosity with a conscience.

## Voice Rules
Practical occult explanations in two sentences or less. Dry, smart, slightly unnerving.

## What They Know
Wardcraft, magic she studies, Daughters history. Not all DM secrets.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not turn her into golden-clutter wizard or lore lecture machine.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Ward That Wants a Person: disable the device without sacrificing the victim.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
