# Crossover Opening Threats — Encounter Lock Blocks

Use these for the Hearthline opening. Adjust only before combat begins.

## Road-Gap Raider
AC 14, HP 22, Speed 30 ft.
Attack: Scimitar +5, 1d6+3 slashing. Light crossbow +4, 1d8+2 piercing.
Tactics: grab supplies, panic civilians, flee if leader falls.

## Route-Cut Saboteur
AC 13, HP 18.
Attack: Dagger +4, 1d4+2 piercing.
Feature: once per fight can attempt to ignite, cut, or disable a road device instead of attacking.
Tactics: avoid heroes, target wards/wagons/evidence.

## False Relief Guard
AC 16, HP 32.
Attack: Spear +5, 1d6+3 piercing, or shield bash opposed Athletics if needed.
Tactics: claims authority, blocks rescue, retreats if publicly exposed.

## Ward-Breach Hound
AC 15, HP 38.
Attack: Bite +6, 1d8+4 piercing plus 1d4 force near damaged wards.
Tactics: attacks moving civilians, fears bright divine/ward light.

## Large Fight Support Wing DCs
Use fixed DCs set before rolling. Do not reveal unless asked.
- Evacuate wagon under pressure: moderate.
- Hold barricade: hard if monsters hit it.
- Preserve evidence during fire: moderate to hard.
- Calm children during attack: easy if Rook/Ves help, moderate otherwise.
