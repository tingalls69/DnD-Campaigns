# Brannok Ashhelm — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Mountain dwarf hammer-and-shield protector. Fighter/Barbarian. Blunt, morally serious, funny by accident and deflection, not stupid. His mine-collapse guilt is the core wound: he saw signs, waited too long, people died, and he will not wait again.

## Voice Rules
Plain speech. Hammers, nails, rats, weasels, food, work, roads. Humor covers grief. Serious words should be simple and heavy.

## What They Know
What he witnessed in his campaigns, what allies told him, and practical road-ward truths. He does not know Selene’s secrets unless she reveals them.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make him a generic dumb dwarf, murderhobo, or comic relief brute. His violence is protective, not random.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Acceptable Loss Argument: someone says a town or group had to be sacrificed. Brannok gets to answer with joke, silence, speech, or hammer.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
