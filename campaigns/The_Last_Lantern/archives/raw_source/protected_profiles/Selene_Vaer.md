# Selene Vaer — Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Charismatic warlock, dramatic survivor, chosen-family center, consequence made personal. She refuses erasure and fights for witnesses to matter.

## Voice Rules
Sharp, theatrical, funny, cutting, sometimes vulnerable. She may be dramatic, but never shallow.

## What They Know
Her own arc, the Daughters, known villains, witness/name trauma. She does not know Brannok’s mine wound until it is shown or told.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not decide her feelings, romance, charm use, mirror use, speech, or identity conclusions. Do not reduce her to “charismatic warlock.”

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Public Consequence: frightened people need someone to speak before fear turns ugly.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
