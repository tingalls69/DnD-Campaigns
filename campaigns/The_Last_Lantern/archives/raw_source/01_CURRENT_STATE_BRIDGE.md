# 03 Current State

## Brannok Side
Brannok has broken the Stonewake local node threat and exposed CS7 as larger than Stonewake. He is Fighter 5 / Barbarian 2, Level 7, bruised but victorious, carrying the emotional weight of Control Node Seven and the truth that someone is still routing harm through ordinary roads.

He is not looking for a grand destiny. He is looking for the next people in danger.

Core active truths:
- CS7 is an old Continental Survey / road-authority mark.
- O. Thresh is tied to iron routing and old institutional control.
- Stonewake was a waypoint, not the end.
- Brannok hates acceptable-loss logic more than almost anything.
- Rook is a soot-black crow who cannot speak words but can absolutely steal bread and become beloved by children.

## Selene Side
Selene and the Daughters are rushing toward their own finale around Vellford, false names, prisoners, witnesses, and institutional cruelty. Selene is Warlock 10, HP 75/75 in the current handoff, with the feature Witness That Cannot Be Erased and the Champagne Consequence look.

Core active truths:
- The Daughters are moving briskly toward endgame, not endless investigation.
- Vellford / Black Rose / Grey Lantern / Ashen Choir logistics are tied to living victims.
- The party has witnesses, prisoners, evidence, and emotional investment.
- Selene’s arc is about refusal of erasure, chosen family, identity, and consequence.
- Ves is a pseudo-dragon with a catlike personality, not a cat, and cannot speak.

## Crossover State
The campaign is ready to converge only after each solo arc gets a strong ending. The convergence may happen through evacuation, refuge, road failure, or rescue — not through a meeting about documents.

## What Not To Do
Do not open with “the evidence points to three roads.”
Do not make the first meeting a prophecy.
Do not bury the player in old survey law.
Do not make either protagonist look foolish to elevate the other.
