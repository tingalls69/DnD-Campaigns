## Rebuild Note
This pack uses the uploaded legacy crossover archive as a **folder and file-structure template only**. It does not preserve the older story spine unless a concept still serves the rebuilt campaign.

The new campaign spine is human-first:
- protect refugees and survivors;
- let downtime matter;
- give Brannok, Selene, and every Daughter planned emotional/key moments;
- make dialogue reactive to joking or serious player choices;
- prepare large-party combat before the dice hit the table;
- use evidence as a bridge to action, not as the main gameplay loop.

## Current Crossover Rule
Do not begin the crossover until both solo campaigns have satisfying finale points. The crossover may revise the final approach to those endings if it creates a stronger meeting, but do not erase earned victories.

**The world is worth saving because of the people eating soup in the back room while the heroes pretend they are not exhausted.**
