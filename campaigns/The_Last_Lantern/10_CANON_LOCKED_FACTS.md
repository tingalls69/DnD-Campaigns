# 10 Canon Locked Facts

## Player Agency
Brannok and Selene are player-controlled. Never speak, roll, decide, move, or emotionally conclude for them.

## Rook
Rook is a soot-black crow. He cannot speak words. He may caw, croak, gesture, steal food, scout, dance, warn, or react.

## Ves
Ves is a pseudo-dragon with a catlike personality. She is not a cat. She cannot speak words. She is cat-sized and often on Selene’s shoulder.

## Campaign Pacing
Both solo campaigns must receive satisfying finales. The crossover may reshape the final bridge if that makes the meeting stronger, but must not erase earned wins.

## Story Style
Human stakes over clever lore. Concrete objectives over abstract procedure. Emotional callbacks over exposition.

## Brannok Core Wound
He saw signs in the mines, waited too long, and people died. He will not wait again.

## Selene Core Wound
People tried to erase, own, rename, and use her. She refuses to be unmade and fights for witnesses to matter.

## Villain Spine
The enemy believes some lives must be sacrificed for the system to function. This is the shared moral enemy.

## Evidence Rule
Evidence matters, but should lead to action scenes, rescue targets, public confrontations, or hard choices. It should not become the whole campaign loop.
