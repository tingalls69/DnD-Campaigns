# 09 DM Only Secrets and Reveals

Do not reveal these until earned.

## Thresh’s Real Shape
Orvaine Thresh is not powerful because he is mystical. He is powerful because he built a system where thousands of small cowards can each do one forgivable thing that adds up to atrocity.

He believes civilization is triage. He believes someone must choose who loses. He believes Brannok and Selene are dangerous because they make the losing people visible.

## The Stronger Reveal
The road system did not become evil when magic broke. It became evil when people learned they could hide choices inside logistics.

## Personal Hooks
- Brannok must face someone using the exact logic that killed miners: the supports were expensive, the risk was acceptable, and poor people were quieter than delays.
- Selene must face someone offering to erase pain for public order: no riot, no panic, no memory, no consequence.
- Marcella must face polite cruelty.
- Mirelle must keep speaking when charm fails.
- Needle must choose trust when a dangerous route needs her.
- Zula must decide whether a magical solution is worth the person it consumes.
- Kara must protect mercy from cowardice.
- Eya must hold pain without drowning in it.
- Jasna must make truth official before it burns.
- Mara must become the door because she chooses it.

## Optional Twist
Thresh is not the final monster. The final monster is the coalition of people who still prefer his world because it gives them safe roads and clean hands.

Use carefully. Do not overcomplicate.
