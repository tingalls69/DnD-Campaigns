# Grey Lantern Route Agent

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 15
- HP 38
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 10 (+0), DEX 16 (+3), CON 12 (+1), INT 14 (+2), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 12 (+1)

## Skills
Stealth +6, Deception +4, Investigation +5, Perception +4

## Attacks
### Shortsword
+6 to hit. Hit: 1d6+3 piercing.

### Hand Crossbow
+6 to hit. Hit: 1d6+3 piercing.

## Features
### Route Slip
Once per scene, the Agent can reveal a prepared escape route, hidden door, cart path, or roof exit if one plausibly exists.

### Signal Lantern
As an action, the Agent can attempt to signal allies. If uninterrupted until the start of their next turn, reinforcements or an escape complication may begin.

## Role
Scout, escape coordinator, logistics link.
