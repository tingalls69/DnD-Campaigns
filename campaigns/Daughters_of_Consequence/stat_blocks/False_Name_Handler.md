# False-Name Handler

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 14
- HP 36
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 10 (+0), DEX 14 (+2), CON 12 (+1), INT 14 (+2), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 16 (+3)

## Skills
Deception +6, Insight +4, Persuasion +6, Religion +4

## Attacks
### Dagger
+5 to hit. Hit: 1d4+2 piercing.

## Features
### Pressure Phrase
One creature within 30 ft. that can hear the Handler must make a Wisdom save DC 14 or have disadvantage on its next ability check before the end of its next turn. This is psychological pressure, not mind control.

### Move the Witness
As a bonus action, the Handler commands one allied guard or enforcer to move up to half speed toward a prisoner, exit, or document.

## Role
Social manipulator and prisoner-transfer coordinator.
