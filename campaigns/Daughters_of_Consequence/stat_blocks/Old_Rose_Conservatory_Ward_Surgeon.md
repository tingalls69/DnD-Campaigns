# Old Rose Conservatory Ward-Surgeon

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 13
- HP 40
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 8 (-1), DEX 12 (+1), CON 14 (+2), INT 17 (+3), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 10 (+0)

## Skills
Arcana +6, Medicine +5, Investigation +6

## Attacks
### Scalpel
+4 to hit. Hit: 1d4+1 piercing.

## Features
### Numbing Draught
Ranged or melee tool attack +4; on hit, target takes no damage but must succeed on CON save DC 14 or have disadvantage on Strength and Dexterity checks until the end of its next turn.

### Stabilize the Asset
As an action, the Ward-Surgeon can stabilize a dying creature or suppress visible distress in a prisoner. This is medically useful but morally suspect.

### Surgical Cowardice
The Ward-Surgeon disengages as a bonus action when adjacent to a Black Rose Enforcer or Guard-Captain.

## Role
Non-frontline medical villain, prisoner-processing threat.
