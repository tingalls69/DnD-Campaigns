# Vellford Escape Cart Driver

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 13
- HP 30
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 12 (+1), DEX 15 (+2), CON 12 (+1), INT 10 (+0), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 9 (-1)

## Skills
Animal Handling +4, Perception +4, Sleight of Hand +5

## Attacks
### Club
+4 to hit. Hit: 1d4+2 bludgeoning.

### Light Crossbow
+4 to hit. Hit: 1d8+2 piercing.

## Features
### Hard Turn
While driving a cart, the driver can force one creature riding or clinging to the cart to make DEX save DC 13 or fall prone in the cart/road.

### Hidden Compartment
The driver’s cart may contain a hidden compartment. Finding it requires a careful search, tool use, or strong suspicion.

## Role
Chase objective, prisoner mover, escape complication.
