# Ashcombe House Guard-Captain

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 17
- HP 58
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 16 (+3), DEX 13 (+1), CON 15 (+2), INT 11 (+0), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 12 (+1)

## Saves
STR +6, CON +5

## Attacks
### Longsword
+6 to hit. Hit: 1d8+3 slashing.

### Multiattack
Makes two longsword attacks.

## Features
### Hold the Hall
Allied guards within 10 ft. gain +1 AC while the captain is conscious and not incapacitated.

### Ordered Retreat
Once per combat, the captain can command up to two allies to move up to half speed without provoking opportunity attacks from one creature of the captain’s choice.

## Role
Tactical guard leader, hallway boss, escape-cover threat.
