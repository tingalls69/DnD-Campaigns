# Session Packet Generator

Before live play, generate or refresh `02_SESSION_PACKET.md` using:

- `03_CURRENT_STATE.md`
- `04_CURRENT_CAST.md`
- `08_ACTIVE_PLOT_THREADS.md`
- `09_RELATIONSHIP_MAP.md`
- Protected character files for anyone present
- The most recent entry in `14_RUNNING_SESSION_LOG.md`
- `12_FUTURE_STORY_PLANS.md` only for direction, not direct reveal

The session packet must:

- Be short enough for ChatGPT to actually use.
- Put relevant character voice notes directly in the packet.
- Include only what matters for the next session.
- Avoid burying the next scene under archive lore.

Recommended packet rhythm:

1. Current scene and immediate pressure.
2. Who is present and what they want.
3. What the player likely cares about right now.
4. What clues, threats, and resources are live.
5. One strong next scene and one alternate path.
6. What to avoid.
