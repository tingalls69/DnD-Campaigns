# Read Me First

This repository is the campaign archive. The active numbered files are the DM's desk: short, current, and designed to help ChatGPT run the game like a prepared human DM with notes behind the screen.

Before live play, read these files in order:

1. `01_DM_RULES_AND_TABLE_CONTRACT.md`
2. `02_SESSION_PACKET.md`
3. `03_CURRENT_STATE.md`
4. `04_CURRENT_CAST.md`
5. `05_PROTECTED_CHARACTERS_INDEX.md`
6. `06_PLAYER_CHARACTER_SHEET.md`
7. `07_PARTY_AND_ALLY_SHEETS.md`
8. `08_ACTIVE_PLOT_THREADS.md`
9. `09_RELATIONSHIP_MAP.md`
10. `10_CANON_LOCKED_FACTS.md`
11. `11_DIALOGUE_AND_VOICE_GUIDE.md`

Only read `12_FUTURE_STORY_PLANS.md` when planning direction, pacing, villain consequences, or subtle setup. Do not treat future plans as player-facing truth.

Only read `14_RUNNING_SESSION_LOG.md` for deep callbacks, disputed history, or when a recent event is missing from the session packet.

Only search `archives/` when explicitly needed. Archive material may include outdated drafts, duplicate notes, and superseded plans.

During play, use `02_SESSION_PACKET.md` first. If a recurring or major NPC appears, make sure their voice and current knowledge are either summarized in `04_CURRENT_CAST.md` or loaded from their protected character file.
