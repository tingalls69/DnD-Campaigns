Character
Role and handling
Selene Vaer
Player character. Half-elf Warlock 9, Great Old One, Pact of the Chain. Gothic, sensual, theatrical, dangerous. Mirror/mask/identity/witness themes. Do not act for her.
Vesper / Ves
Black violet-eyed pseudodragon familiar. Catlike affection, pride, possessiveness, and fierce protection. Scout, warning sense, Help/sting when commanded.
Kara of the Aureum
Human Cleric 9, Life Domain. Slightly celestial/Aureum presence. Direct, protective, disciplined, oath-bound. Prioritizes victims, children, undead threats, healing, and witness survival.
Eya Harisa
Human Cleric 8, Knowledge Domain. Gentle, witness-focused healer and investigator. Good with trauma, quiet care, thought-reading support, and vulnerable survivors.
Jasna
Human Rogue 9, Mastermind. Legal/evidence mind. Turns chaos into records, maps, timelines, contradictions, weak points, and leverage. Brilliant but physically vulnerable.
Zula Ozu
Tiefling Wizard 9, Divination. Ink-stained tattoo-artist arcane specialist. Practical, dry, precise. Reads marks, route seams, wards, and ritual logic.
Marcella Viretti
Human Bard 8, Lore. Elegant social weapon. Cutting, aristocratic, emotionally insightful, socially lethal. Poor fit for mud/survival. Selene romance is active.
Sewing Needle
Tabaxi Rogue 9, Thief. Scout, traps, locks, rooftops, criminal entrances, rope, knots, pursuit, and irreverent survival instinct.
Mara
Human Fighter 5, Battle Master style. Scarred lower-camp muscle. Sword/shield, intimidation, prisoner guard, blunt protection, direct physical action.
Relationship chemistry
Selene and Marcella: now explicitly romantic/emotional after Hillfort. They discussed the masquerade, Odessa, the missed goodbye kiss, and shared a delayed first kiss. Marcella should be tender when appropriate but still witty, proud, and socially lethal.
Selene and Ves: Ves is emotionally protective, jealous, and proud. She can sense danger through the bond before others notice. She is not a prop.
Selene and Kara: Kara respects Selene but will oppose cruelty or reckless mirror/charm use from care and principle.
Selene and Zula: Zula is blunt about magical safety and may forbid unsafe experiments until procedures exist.
Selene and Needle: Needle favors clever approaches, criminal entrances, scouting, and practical loyalty without ownership.
Selene and Mara: Mara respects clarity and strength. She is protective, direct, and less subtle than the others.
Selene and Jasna: Jasna protects facts and consequences; she will preserve agency by making the record matter.
Selene and Eya: Eya centers victims, trauma, and survival costs. She is gentle but not weak.
