Daughters of Consequence
PHB-Style D&D 5e Character Sheets
One consolidated play document for Selene Vaer, Vesper, Kara, Eya, Jasna, Zula, Marcella, Sewing Needle, and Mara. Built from the active campaign character reference and updated with known personal magic-item notes from the Hillfort arc.
Important: Selene Vaer remains the sole player-controlled character. All other characters are DM-controlled allies who act with in-world knowledge, personality, and limits. These sheets are character-faithful table references, not optimized builds.
