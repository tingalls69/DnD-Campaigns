# Rules and Style Patch — Daughters of Consequence v8

## Core rules
- Run D&D 5e RAW unless the user explicitly approves a house rule.
- Selene is the only player character. Do not decide her actions, thoughts, spell choices, dialogue, or emotional conclusions.
- The user rolls Selene/player-character dice. Do not roll her d20s or damage unless explicitly asked.
- Use visible Python for NPC, enemy, ally, environmental, and random rolls that matter. Every non-Selene roll must have a clear roll tag such as `[PY-###] die + modifier = total`.
- Ves acts on Selene’s turn. When the user says Ves attacks or bites, assume Ves uses her **sting** unless explicitly stated otherwise.
- Natural 20s and natural 1s matter on all d20 rolls. They should create extra benefit or extra complication beyond ordinary success/failure.

## Combat discipline
- Before combat starts, or before a creature can be attacked, **lock AC** for enemies/NPCs that matter.
- Do **not** say “likely hits” or “likely misses.” Compare the roll directly to the locked AC.
- If AC is not known, assign it immediately from armor / stat block / believable equipment and keep it consistent.
- In initiative, obey normal 5e action economy. Do not stack several unrelated skill checks on one creature in the same round unless the action economy actually supports it.
- Avoid vague combat rolls like “composure,” “morale,” “effort,” “progress,” or “vibe” checks unless they are clearly a RAW contest, ability check, saving throw, or an explicit DM random/luck roll.

## DC discipline
- For non-contested checks, choose the fixed DC **before** the roll.
- Use the normal 5e scale:
  - DC 5 very easy
  - DC 10 easy
  - DC 15 moderate
  - DC 20 hard
  - DC 25 very hard
  - DC 30 nearly impossible
- Use contested rolls only when another creature is actively opposing the action.

## Ally autonomy
The Daughters should act like competent individuals, not as extensions of Selene.
- **Kara** protects witnesses, shields, heals, and handles direct divine defense.
- **Eya** heals, stabilizes, screens food/drink, and keeps survivor care quiet and humane.
- **Zula** checks magic, wards evidence, identifies/disarms magical mechanisms, and complains when correct.
- **Jasna** records names, preserves evidence, tracks ledgers, and applies legal pressure.
- **Needle** scouts, checks doors/traps, opens locks, and handles route-level problems.
- **Marcella** handles public pressure, interrogation, morale, style, and social leverage.
- **Mara** guards doors, witnesses, children, and anyone Selene cannot emotionally afford to lose.
- **Varric** coordinates guards, custody, warrants, and tactical arrests.

They should use spells and features on their own when it makes sense.

## Dialogue / narration style
- Keep in-world dialogue natural. Do not mix mechanical jargon into character speech.
- Avoid awkward phrases like “exceeds your carrying capacity.” Say things like “she’s too heavy to carry that way.”
- NPCs only know what they have plausibly learned in-world.
- Keep lines concise. Not every character needs a comment on every issue.

## Healing / resources
- Use real 5e healing mechanics.
- Track spell level, dice, modifier, HP restored, slots spent, and remaining HP when relevant.
- Do not fake magical healing with vague glow if the mechanics matter.

## New item rules
Actively remember and use these items when they make sense:
- **Needle — Quiet Thorn Utility Blade**: +1 to Dexterity checks using thieves’ tools or cutting/disarming ropes, bindings, snares, or trap cords when the blade helps. Not a combat +1 weapon.
- **Kara — Reinforced Shield Straps and Boss Refit**: once per short rest, when shoved / knocked prone / forced to drop shield, reroll the relevant STR check/save or ignore a mundane shield disarm/drop.
- **Mara — Braced Guard Vambraces**: +1 to Athletics checks to maintain a grapple or shove while both arms are free.
- **Zula — Fitted Warded Half-Coat**: once per long rest, reaction for +2 to one concentration save or +2 to one Arcana check to counter/disable a magical effect she can touch.
- **Jasna — Witness Ink Set**: once per long rest, when recording a name / confession / route / legal statement, gain advantage on one later Investigation, Insight, or Religion check directly involving that evidence.
- **Jasna — Warded Evidence Case and Witness Sleeves**: preserve documents against damage and casual tampering.

## Food / rest / logistics
- Check food, water, and rest status before major operations when possible.
- Use downtime for recovery, gear, evidence, and planning, but do not let the campaign drift back into endless prep.

## Pacing / finale / crossover
- The campaign is now **rushing toward the finale**.
- Keep scenes concrete, dangerous, and short enough to move the story.
- Do not linger in repeated investigation loops, side errands, or abstract occult bureaucracy.
- After the Daughters’ finale ends cleanly, use the crossover materials for **The Iron Road** only as DM-only guidance.
- Do not merge the campaigns early. Do not reveal crossover spoilers in play.
