# v5 Story Skeleton Addendum — Grey Lantern Operation

## Immediate next arc beat

The next concrete objective is the **Grey Lantern operation** tomorrow night, not more abstract occult investigation.

Core truth:
- The culvert with the grey lantern mark is bait.
- The true handoff is at the dry ford half a mile south.
- The shipment is names, identity papers, and service conversions tied to Vellford.
- The silver-capped cane receiver is the next major link.

## Agreed operation structure

1. **Decoy Culvert Team** — Commander Rhea Varric and trusted guards make the culvert discovery look real and pressure the bait handoff.
2. **Dry Ford Protection Team** — Selene, Kara, Eya, Zula, Jasna, Marcella, and guards prioritize captives and papers. Victims first, papers second, prisoners third unless prisoners threaten either.
3. **Capture Net** — Needle and Mara hide along the receiver's likely escape path. They delay/catch the receiver if practical but must signal rather than die proving a point.
4. **Selene Floater Role** — Selene starts on the protection side. If Needle/Mara signal capture failure or receiver escape, Selene may Dimension Door to their last known position. Once she does, dry ford protection must hold without her.

## Signal system

Primary: Ves relay. Needle/Mara show Ves two fingers dragged across the throat, then point to the escape path. Ves relays urgent bond impression to Selene: capture failing / come now.

Backup: short double raven/reed click.

Backup visible: low silver flash twice near ground.

## Keep the operation grounded

This should be a tactical ambush/rescue, not phrase-law or abstract road metaphysics. Concrete objectives:
- protect living captives;
- prevent destruction of name papers;
- stop handlers;
- catch or identify silver-capped cane receiver;
- discover Vellford identity-laundering thread;
- avoid being fooled by the culvert decoy.

## Loot / reward direction

After Grey Lantern or before a later big boss, provide tangible rewards: seized coin, practical upgraded gear, rose/initial daggers, better tools, armor upgrades, spell components, protective clothing, and modest utility items. Plan a larger-town shopping/prep day before the next major boss/finale.

# v6 Next Arc Direction: Vellford

After Grey Lantern, the Path of Consequence points toward **Vellford**. Vellford should be a concrete investigation/action arc about false service contracts, stolen-name laundering, missing people, corrupt estate officials, and recoverable records. The key personal hook is the missing girl using Needle's old name, **Nessa Vale**.

Avoid turning Vellford into an abstract metaphysical puzzle. Useful scene objectives: infiltrate a records office, protect a witness, confront a false-service broker, rescue a hidden servant, stop papers from being burned, catch a courier, expose a house agent, or force a corrupt official to name the buyer.

The silver-capped cane and Cane-Signatory are important but should be handled through concrete evidence and interrogation. The cane can verify/bind identity through reflection/name recognition, but do not make it the entire plot engine.
