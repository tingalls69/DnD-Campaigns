# Vellford Dawn and Endgame

The party is at **Saint Orveth’s Reliquary** at dawn, packed and ready. This is the last stretch before the finale.

## Immediate plan
- Leave at dawn for Vellford.
- Keep the pace fast.
- Prioritize rescue and evidence over clever detours.
- Use the established split if needed:
  - **Rescue team:** Kara, Eya, Marcella, Zula, plus reliquary guards.
  - **Records team:** Needle, Mara, Selene, Jasna, plus reliquary guards.

## Vellford objectives
1. **Ashcombe House** — rescue living victims / recover anyone still held there.
2. **Old Rose Conservatory** — seize Mavren Solt’s transfer index / records if not already fully extracted.
3. **Vellford Domestic Benevolence Office** — expose the public-facing mask of the operation.
4. **Road-use file** — DM-only logistics thread, kept as a later reveal if the story naturally reaches it.

## Endgame rule
Do not let the campaign drift. Every scene should either:
- rescue someone,
- expose a paper trail,
- capture a villain,
- or move the party closer to the finale.

After this, the Daughters’ arc should end cleanly, and then the Iron Road crossover can begin from the finished state.
