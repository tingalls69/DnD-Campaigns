Unresolved Threads, Deep Lore, and Arc Planning
Future hooks and DM-only guidance
Deep lore safety
Reveal mirror, charm, patron, and Witness Road lore slowly through play. Selene knows pieces, not the full truth.
No mirror scene should happen without explicit player choice. Never force Selene to use the Argent Glass.
The mirror and charm should create temptation, procedure, risk, and discovery, not random punishment.
Personal threads should support the main adventure rather than replacing it unless the user chooses to pivot.
NPCs should not know Selene’s private Odessa backstory unless she reveals it in-world.
Mirror, charm, and patron
Element
Current truth / future use
Closed-Eye Charm
Protective inheritance from Mara Vell. Closes intrusion and remembers who tried. Learn it by testing harmless intrusions first: minor charms, illusions, mind-touching spells from trusted allies, while recording reactions. Never start with the mirror.
Argent Glass
Dangerous mirror/door through the Shadowfell or something using it as a hall. Opens to the Witness Behind Glass. Do not use by candlelight; do not reflect Selene and another person at once; do not ask who is watching; do not uncover under fear/grief/desperation/anger.
Witness Behind Glass
Distinct from Selene’s patron. Seeks Selene because of the mirror and because the mirror chose not to stay lost. Name is dangerous with wanting and worst before glass.
Selene’s patron
Witness Beneath the Root / Closed Eye Behind Glass. Unknown to most, not simply evil, not easily researched in libraries. Name is a witness mark, not a lure. Tied to names, witnesses, debts, roots, reflections, identity.
Darkstar’s warning
The mirror needs reflection, invitation, or desperation. Deny all three. Darkstar is useful but dangerous and unrepentant.
Unresolved story threads
Darkstar transfer or containment remains volatile. Road forces may attempt rescue, erasure, assassination, or extraction.
Dovan/Voll second ledger may contain additional routes, victim categorizations, old chambers, and future threats.
Hollow Wood victim tokens remain a cleanup thread requiring Kalviache, Zula, Kara, and maybe families.
Hillfort’s route network is disabled but historically important. Some routes may remain mapped/warded rather than destroyed.
The Unremembered Saint is the Arc 4 primary threat: erasure-as-mercy, names removed to spare pain, witnesses rewritten or emptied.
The Ashen Choir knows Selene’s true name and uses ash-road magic, Hollow Witnesses, and road-stones.
Liora Vale remains central: a Hollow Witness tied to Darkstar, the Saint, and the first successful disappearances.
C. and House Tavaril remain unresolved but should not overtake Arc 4 unless chosen.
Caerwyn Tavaril remains alive, humiliated, hostile, and likely to act through legal/social/indirect force first.
Selene and Marcella’s relationship matters but should not make every scene about romance. Use it for trust, risk, comfort, disagreement, and callbacks.

Recommended arc frames
Darkstar Convoy / Temple Transfer: move Darkstar to Saint Orveth’s Reliquary under strict precautions while the road tries to open.
Ledger Beneath the Stove: Voll’s hidden ledger leads to another town, route node, or old estate before someone cleans it up.
Closed-Eye Procedure: Zula, Kara, Marcella, Jasna, and Selene design a careful charm test. The first memory points toward Mara, Aurelian, or a prior intrusion.
Hollow Wood Cleanup: Kalviache asks for help releasing the split-oak tokens and sealing or calming a deeper root.
Mirror Wants Out: the Argent Glass stays covered, but a nearby reflection imitates Selene, Marcella, Ves, or a lost voice. The party must investigate without reckless mirror use.
Witness Road Estate / Temple: a noble ruin, mercy camp, or forgotten witness-temple becomes a combat/investigation node with moral choices around voluntary erasure.
Suggested beat rhythm
Combat/action beat with objective beyond HP.
Fallout or character scene with only the two or three most relevant Daughters speaking.
Investigation or choice that moves toward multiple possible nodes.
Combat/action beat that tests a different character pressure.
Downtime/interlude checkpoint where the outline changes based on Selene’s choices, casualties, alliances, and relationship shifts.
Things to avoid
Forcing mirror use or patron revelations as cutscenes.
Making C. or House Tavaril dominate unless the user chooses that route.
Letting NPC allies solve mysteries or combats without Selene’s choices mattering.
Turning the Daughters into archetype buttons instead of distinct people.
Long legal proceedings that replace adventure pacing.
Having Darkstar monologue freely; he should be controlled, dangerous, and strategic.
