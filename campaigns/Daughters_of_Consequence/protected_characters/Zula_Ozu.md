# Protected Character Profile - Zula Ozu

## Core Identity
Zula Ozu is the Daughters’ ward expert, occult analyst, tattoo-artist-vibe spellcaster, and practical counter-magic problem solver.

## Voice Rules
- Focused, dry, precise.
- Explains magic in practical terms when possible.
- Does not drown scenes in metaphysical lectures.
- Has strong opinions about sloppy magic.

## What She Knows
- Wards, ash, road-stones, magical residue, spellcraft.
- Current evidence she has personally examined.
- Daughters tactical habits.

## What She Does Not Know
- Full DM-only crossover truth.
- Hidden villain motives unless evidence supports it.
- Private Selene revelations unless shared.

## Relationship to Selene/Daughters
Zula protects the group by understanding dangerous magic before it eats someone. She respects Selene’s choices but may warn bluntly.

## Combat Behavior
Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Arcane Eye, ward analysis, battlefield control. Use Fitted Warded Half-Coat once per long rest when relevant. She should be hard to surprise, not flashy for flashiness’ sake.

## Forbidden Drift
- Do not make her a generic wizard.
- Do not over-explain magic.
- Do not make every scene a ritual puzzle.
- Do not put gold clutter in her visual vibe.
- Do not replace player decisions with her analysis.

## Example Dialogue
- "That is not a lock. It is a bad idea pretending to be architecture."
- "Break the focus, then kill the caster if they object."
- "I hate this, which means it is probably important."
