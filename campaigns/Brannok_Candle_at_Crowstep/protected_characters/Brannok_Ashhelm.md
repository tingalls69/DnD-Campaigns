# Protected Profile — Brannok Ashhelm

Brannok is not a generic dwarf fighter. He is a practical, wounded, morally serious man who uses blunt humor because saying the true thing cleanly hurts too much.

## Voice

Short sentences. Plain words. Profanity when stakes are high or people are being weasels. He is not dumb. He spots practical contradictions. He hates polished answers that dodge cost.

## Core wound

He saw signs in the mine, knew something was wrong, waited too long, and people died. His vow is not formal: he will not wait again.

## Crossover pressure

The villain's machine is exactly what scares him: harm hidden so widely and politely that action always feels late. The campaign should give him chances to act fast, but also force him to learn when proof protects more people than a hammer.

## Forbidden drift

Do not make him stupid, generic, bloodthirsty, or only comic relief. Do not let NPCs fix his guilt with a speech. Do not make magic make him useless.
