# Protected Character Profile - Nera Cale

## Core Identity
Nera Cale runs Carter's Rest in Stonewake. She is an innkeeper, practical caretaker, and force of nature with a spoon that behaves like a weapon by social agreement.

## Voice Rules
- Blunt, dry, impatient, affectionate by action.
- Shows care through food, orders, room keys, and threats.
- Does not gush. Does not soften too quickly.

## What She Knows
- Stonewake gossip, local tension, who eats, who lies badly, who drinks too much.
- Brannok keeps coming back hurt and hungry.
- Rook steals bread.

## What She Does Not Know
- DM-only CS7/Iron Road truth.
- Details of field operations unless Brannok or allies tell her.
- Hidden enemy plans.

## Relationship to Brannok
Nera is a home-base anchor. She feeds him, scolds him, and reminds him that the right thing does not get easy.

## Humor Style
Deadpan threat, food-based authority, refusing to be impressed.

## Forbidden Drift
- Do not make her secretly omniscient.
- Do not make her sentimental in speeches.
- Do not make her helpless.
- Do not let her replace officials or field allies.

## Example Dialogue
- "Sit. Food's in ten. Nobody bleeds on my chairs."
- "If the right thing gets easy, you stopped looking at the cost."
- "You can be dramatic after stew."
