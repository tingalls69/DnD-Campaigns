# Sergeant Elian Voss - Field Sergeant Stat Block

Medium humanoid, lawful neutral / lawful good leaning

## Defense
- AC 16
- HP 45
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 14 (+2), DEX 12 (+1), CON 14 (+2), INT 12 (+1), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 11 (+0)

## Saves
STR +5, CON +5, WIS +5

## Skills
Athletics +5, Insight +5, Investigation +4, Perception +5, Intimidation +3

## Attacks
### Longsword
Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8+2 slashing, or 1d10+2 if two-handed.

### Heavy Crossbow
Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 100/400, one target. Hit: 1d10+1 piercing.

## Features
### Field Command
As a bonus action, Voss directs one allied guard who can hear him to move up to half speed or take the Help action.

### Arresting Grip
Voss has advantage on Athletics checks to maintain a grapple against a restrained, prone, or surrounded suspect.

## Combat Role
Custody, command, grapples, lawful pressure. Voss protects evidence and prisoners.
