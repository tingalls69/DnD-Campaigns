# Ward Lancer - Survey Spear Enforcer

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 15
- HP 44
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 14 (+2), DEX 16 (+3), CON 14 (+2), INT 11 (+0), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 9 (-1)

## Saves
DEX +6, CON +5

## Skills
Athletics +5, Perception +4, Stealth +6

## Attacks
### Ward Spear
Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8+3 piercing.

### Short Sword
Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6+3 piercing.

## Features
### Line Guard
Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from the Ward Lancer when they move from within 10 ft. to within 5 ft. of him.

### Cable Watch
The Lancer has advantage on Perception checks to notice creatures approaching active ward-cables or node devices.

## Combat Role
Reach control, bodyguard, cable defender.
