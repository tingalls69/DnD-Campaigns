# Pellan Orsik - Dwarf Smith Ally

Medium humanoid, dwarf

## Defense
- AC 17
- HP 52
- Speed 25 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 16 (+3), DEX 10 (+0), CON 16 (+3), INT 13 (+1), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 10 (+0)

## Saves
STR +6, CON +6

## Skills
Athletics +6, Insight +4, Investigation +4, Smith's Tools +7

## Attacks
### Smith Hammer
Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8+3 bludgeoning.

### Heavy Tongs
Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6+3 bludgeoning. On hit, Pellan may attempt to shove or pin a loose object as part of the same action if fiction supports it.

## Features
### Old Metal Eye
Pellan has advantage on checks to identify forged, re-stamped, condemned, or sabotaged metalwork.

### Hold the Frame
As a reaction when an adjacent ally hits an object, construct, or ward-device, Pellan can brace the target, adding +1d4 damage to that hit.

## Combat Role
Durable smith, object-break support, old road-metal recognition.
