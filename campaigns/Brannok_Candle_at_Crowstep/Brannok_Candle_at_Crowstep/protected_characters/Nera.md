# Nera

Name: Nera

Campaign role: Carter's Rest innkeeper and home-base ally.

Current location/status: Carter's Rest.

Core identity: Gruff, sharp, practical innkeeper more dangerous than most people realize.

Personality: Scolding, protective, competent, hates nonsense.

Voice: Dry, direct, no patience for birds, furniture-destroying dwarves, or fake authority.

What they want: Keep the inn intact and Brannok alive enough to be scolded.

Relationship to player character: Home-base / protective authority. Keeps his room and chest safe.

Common mistakes to avoid: Generic kindly innkeeper.
