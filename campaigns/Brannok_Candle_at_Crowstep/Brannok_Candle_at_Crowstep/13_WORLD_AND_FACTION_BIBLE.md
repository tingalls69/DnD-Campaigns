# World And Faction Bible

## Locations

### Crowstep / Carter's Rest

Carter's Rest is Brannok's home base. Nera keeps his room and chest safe.

### Stonewake

Current final-arc location. Road-ward corruption, false repairs, route lies, survey-store cache, and final road-control node all converge here.

### East Ridge Survey Store

Current scene location. Cache/staging point for ward-iron wedges, route material, and old survey-stock.

### South Toll Cut

Site of bell-lock / chain-heart road breach and half-breached creature.

## Factions And Groups

### Ward Office / Ward Hall

Official road authority. Compromised in places, but Voss represents practical lawful pressure.

### Road-Control Network

A system of old road-ward parts, false repairs, route ciphers, and people who know how to make a road lie.

## World Rules

- Road-ward systems have physical, magical, and bureaucratic components.
- False safety is a recurring villain method.
- Bad paperwork can kill as surely as bad supports.

## History And Lore

Brannok left the mines after a preventable collapse caused by corrupt owners using cheap materials, bad supports, and falsified safety records. Before he understood the full truth, he had been used as muscle to keep workers quiet. This is the moral engine of the campaign.
