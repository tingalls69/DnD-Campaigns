## Roleplay Core
Brannok is blunt, practical, morally serious, and not stupid. He is suspicious of polished authority and fake safety. He prefers simple language and concrete action. His rage is not mindless violence; it is a disciplined protective response that should be portrayed as the shield deciding when the hammer moves.
