# 09 Future Crossover and Daughters Compatibility Notes

This document remains **DM-only planning** for the eventual post-finale crossover between Brannok / Crowstep and Selene / Daughters of Consequence. It must not alter current Stonewake pacing, Brannok's finale, or the Daughters' own finale.

## Non-negotiables
- Do **not** merge the campaigns before both solo finales are satisfying.
- Do **not** make either protagonist a secondary character in the crossover.
- Preserve player agency, player-controlled Brannok dice, visible non-player rolls, and strict NPC knowledge boundaries.
- Keep the crossover hidden until naturally relevant after both finales.
- Preserve the user's preference for gradual fantasy escalation: start grounded, then rise into magical stakes through concrete anchors.

## Important compatibility note
The user likes investigation **when it rewards connections to things already known**. Brannok should get “oh, that guy is bad?” moments from recognizing timing, routes, names, marks, and contradictions — not from being fed a random new clue pile.

## DM-only road thread to preserve
When it fits naturally, have Mirelle find ward-iron in the Carrow ledger that was redirected under a cipher that does not match Stonewake:

**▲ | CS7 | RW-ROUTING | IRON TRANSFER AUTHORIZED — O. THRESH, COMMISSIONER (RET.)**

- The name/cipher should mean nothing at first.
- If Brannok shows it to Pellan, Pellan recognizes it as an obsolete road-warden accounting mark.
- Do not resolve it during the solo finale. It is a thread pointing out of Stonewake.

## Crossover shape after both finales
The crossover should rise from:
- road control,
- fake safety charms,
- forged route papers,
- ward-iron smuggling,
- witness protection,
- and road wards that have become political tools.

The final combined campaign can gradually add:
- more visible spells,
- more non-human ancestries,
- more classed NPCs,
- more magical road threats,
- and a genuinely high-fantasy scale.

## Brannok-specific note
Brannok’s solo finale should finish Stonewake cleanly. Only after that should the crossover begin to pull him toward a larger road-control network that extends beyond the city.
