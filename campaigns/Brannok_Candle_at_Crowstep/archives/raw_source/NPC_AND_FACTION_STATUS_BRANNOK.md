# NPC and Faction Status - Brannok / Candle at Crowstep

DM-FACING. Use this to track who knows what, who trusts Brannok, who is in custody, and which NPCs have active motives. Do not reveal hidden/private motives unless earned in play.

## Brannok's Immediate Circle
### Rook
- Soot-black crow, rude, clever, loyal on terms only he understands.
- Functions as scout/alarm bird, not a magical familiar unless later established.
- Likes bread and cheese; was denied cheese at the North Road wayhouse.
- Current location: with Brannok in Dessa's wagon at the wayhouse.

### Dessa Marn
- Merchant bound for Stonewake with lamp oil, wool, and private crates.
- Sharp-eyed, practical, dry, socially capable, and good at reading roads/people/cargo marks.
- Not a frontline fighter; can keep low, run if told, and shout useful information.
- Saw Brannok protect a wounded driver, capture cleanup thieves, recover stolen cargo, and choose safety over baiting a buyer in the dark.
- Current attitude toward Brannok: respectful and pragmatic trust.
- Current location: North Road wayhouse with Brannok/Jory.
- Knowledge: knows about the three-cut marker ambush, old tollhouse recovery, green-lantern buyer, and Brannok's Crowstep reputation in broad terms.

### Jory
- Younger driver with Dessa.
- Better with horses and wheels than combat.
- Helped at the wagon, resisted the knife-woman's attempt to grab him, and returned Brannok's handaxe from the wagon sideboard.
- Current attitude: nervous respect; grateful Brannok is on their side.
- Current location: North Road wayhouse.

## Crowstep Allies and Contacts
### Calder Rusk
- Crowstep watch captain.
- Lawful, practical, dry, increasingly trusts Brannok as useful trouble.
- Gave Brannok travel writ, sealed evidence packet, and note to Sergeant Elian Voss in Stonewake.
- Does not know north-road wayhouse events yet unless road guards later report them.

### Mara Vell
- Quartermaster authority.
- Precise, stern, competent; hates sloppy records because records can protect or kill people.
- Gave Brannok a quartermaster seal-strip and warning about Stonewake's safety trade.
- Released 18 gp additional seizure share to Brannok.
- Does not know north-road wayhouse events yet.

### Brenna
- Copper Ladle keeper.
- Practical, sharp, tolerant of Brannok and Rook within limits.
- Gave Brannok 5 sp credit toward a future room for unused lodging.
- Accepted two fresh wolf carcasses for possible stew credit.
- Does not know north-road wayhouse events.

### Sister Vale
- Healer committed to keeping people alive regardless of guilt, faction, or origin.
- Dry, stern, practical, protective.
- Important rule: do not portray her as casually wanting prisoners dead/buried.

### Garron Flint
- Old dwarven smith west of Crowstep near lime kilns.
- Refused to sell old ward iron to Crest.
- Repaired Brannok's handaxe and fitted the ward-iron shield boss.
- Holding recovered ward-iron crate safely until Mara comes for it.
- Respects Brannok enough to trust him with the shield upgrade.
- Does not know facts he was not told. Avoid knowledge bleed.

### Darik Venn
- Garron's former apprentice.
- Sold old ward iron to Crest foolishly, then hid back one crate after realizing danger.
- Brannok persuaded him to cooperate and return to Garron with proof.
- Current status: sorting/labeling ward iron under Garron's hard supervision.
- Brannok advocated for accountability rather than simple punishment.

### Tovan Reed
- Flour wagon driver rescued by Brannok on the east road.
- His sister asked Brannok for help in Crowstep.
- Alive, returned to town with wagon; owes gratitude, not coin by Brannok's insistence.

### Skrit
- Older goblin from the east-road rocks.
- Negotiated with Brannok in Goblin.
- Agreed to leave travelers alone and possibly approach the guard for work.
- Has food/coin for a few days due to Brannok's deal.
- Relationship: cautious respect; Brannok kept his word.
