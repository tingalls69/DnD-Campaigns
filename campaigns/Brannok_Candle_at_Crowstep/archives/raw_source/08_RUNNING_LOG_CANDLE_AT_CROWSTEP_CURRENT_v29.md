# 08 Running Log — Candle at Crowstep Current

## Current state recap
Brannok has entered the final local stretch of the Stonewake arc. The story has moved from grounded corruption into visible road-ward fantasy: false ward-gates, bell-locks, ward-hounds, road-binders, old road-iron, route ciphers, and a final local cache in the survey store.

## Key late-arc beats
### Ward Hall
- Brannok went to Ward Hall unarmed and unarmored to answer accusations about property destruction and violence.
- He gave a clean, direct summary of the prior road-ward sabotage case from his own perspective.
- He specifically pointed out the suspicious timing of Halven’s objections, and the fact that Halven and the hired guard used the words “children” and “cages” before Brannok or the Ward Office introduced those facts.
- The interim ruling kept the red ledger and seized cargo in Ward custody and suspended Halven.

### Old bridge ward-gate
- Brannok, Kaelis, Voss, Pellan, Tovin, and Rook found a false bracket replacing real ward-gate iron.
- Ward-hounds and a Glimmerjaw came through the damaged gate.
- Brannok used the Ashhelm Road-Hammer and Ward-Iron Brace to pin and then break Glimmerjaw's escape logic.
- The false bracket was removed and the gate was temporarily stabilized.

### Red Willow Crossing
- The repair batch included Red Willow Crossing.
- A grain-and-medicine caravan was forced into a bad approach.
- Brannok shouted for the caravan to jump clear.
- The wagon carried a hidden sabotage crate: real ward-gate teeth and false-ward powder.
- The crate also contained a hidden “return route box” setup.
- This pointed to a larger road-control system using medicine and official-looking cargo as cover.

### South Toll Cut
- Brannok, Garrow, Voss, Kaelis, Pellan, and Tovin raced to South Toll Cut when the dead bell started striking.
- The bell-lock and chain-heart were opening a road breach for a half-breached creature.
- Brannok hit the lower chain pin, broke the lock, and the breach shut.
- Garrow was revealed as an old road-binder who had helped build part of the lie and was then tethered/coerced into helping stop it.
- A huge claw fragment remained as evidence; Brannok dragged a lashed claw-talon back to town on broken toll planks with Rook riding it like a banner.

### The current survey-store fight
- Brannok and the field team found the **East Ridge Survey Store** being used as another cache.
- A gray-coated route woman was guarding ward-iron wedges and route material.
- A tarp-covered stitched shape was hidden in the back.
- Brannok hit the gray-coated woman hard, then finished her.
- Kaelis, Voss, Pellan, and Tovin kept the room under control.
- The tarp-covered shape was exposed and beaten down.
- The room was secured, revealing more ward-iron, route materials, and a likely final node for the local conspiracy.

## Current save point
Brannok is at the **East Ridge Survey Store**, with the room controlled and the local evidence/cache exposed. The story should move quickly toward the final local confrontation rather than returning to a slow investigation cycle.

## Tone notes
- The campaign is headed for its ending.
- Continue to reward callbacks and known names/marks.
- Do not slow down with random prisoner questioning.
- Let the evidence point directly to the final local node.
- More magic and classed enemies are welcome; ordinary humans with knives should no longer dominate the danger.
