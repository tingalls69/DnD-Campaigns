# 07 Active Outline and Next Options

## Current Arc Position
The campaign is in the **final local act** of Stonewake.

The investigation phase has already paid off:
- Ward Hall exposed the timing issues and the red ledger.
- The old bridge ward-gate revealed false repair parts.
- Red Willow Crossing exposed hidden sabotage kits in medicine crates.
- South Toll Cut revealed the bell-lock / chain-heart breach mechanism.
- The survey store exposed a local cache of ward-iron wedges and route material.

This is no longer about “find another clue.” It is about **finishing the local road-control conspiracy** and moving to the crossover only after the solo finale is complete.

## Immediate Story Goal
From the survey store:
1. Secure the room and evidence.
2. Recover any route marks / ledgers / ward-iron wedges / hidden cache material.
3. Identify the last local node or handoff point.
4. Move directly to the finale, not back into a clue loop.

## Story Shape / Priority
- Prefer concrete objectives over abstract investigation.
- Prefer enemies with classed abilities or magical roadcraft over generic knife men.
- Keep the fantasy dial rising gradually.
- Brannok's rage should be increasingly framed as a deliberate protective tool.
- The remaining local conflict should be understandable in one sentence:
  “A road-control network is using false repairs, old ward-iron, and route ciphers to control travel and exploit people.”

## What to avoid
- Do not send Brannok on random prisoner-questioning errands.
- Do not create a pile of unrelated names.
- Do not slow down for procedural accounting unless it directly creates a final lead.
- Do not merge the crossover early.

## DM-only seed to preserve
If the Carrow ledger appears in play, the ward-iron was redirected under the obsolete cipher:
**▲ | CS7 | RW-ROUTING | IRON TRANSFER AUTHORIZED — O. THRESH, COMMISSIONER (RET.)**
It means nothing to the player at first. If shown to Pellan, he recognizes it as an obsolete road-warden accounting mark from retired road work. Do not resolve this immediately; it is just a thread pointing out of Stonewake.

## Endgame readiness
The current scene should quickly pivot to the final Stonewake confrontation, then hand off to the crossover aftermath only after Brannok has a satisfying ending.
