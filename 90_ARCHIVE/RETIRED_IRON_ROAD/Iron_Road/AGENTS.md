# Campaign Repository Rules

- Preserve canon from Brannok, Daughters, and Iron Road sources.
- Never start the crossover until both solo finales and final save states are documented.
- Never flatten the crossover into one giant file.
- Keep active play files short and useful.
- Treat `02_SESSION_PACKET.md` as the live DM desk.
- Protected characters must match their profiles and source campaign canon.
- Never let future plans become current truth.
- Never let one protagonist erase the other's agency or spotlight.
- After every session, refresh `02_SESSION_PACKET.md`, `03_CURRENT_STATE.md`, and `04_CURRENT_CAST.md` first.
