# Opening Threat Stat Blocks - Mirren's Crossing

These are simple, table-speed 5e-compatible enemies. Adjust only after confirming party level/final states.

## Road-Cutter

Medium humanoid, AC 14, HP 27, Speed 30 ft.

STR +3, DEX +1, CON +2, INT +0, WIS +0, CHA -1

Attacks:
- Pry Axe: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 slashing.
- Hook and Drag: +5 Athletics contested by target Athletics/Acrobatics; on success pull target 10 ft. or knock prone.

Tactics: break evidence, shove people off bridge edges, target rescuers.

## Ledger-Cleaner

Medium humanoid, AC 13, HP 22, Speed 30 ft.

STR +0, DEX +2, CON +1, INT +3, WIS +1, CHA +1

Attacks:
- Dagger: +4 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing.
- Fire Oil Flask: +4 to hit, range 20/60, target object or 5-ft square ignites; creature entering/starting there takes 1d6 fire until extinguished.

Tactics: burn records, flee, lie plausibly.

## Cracked-Lantern Wardwright

Medium humanoid, AC 15, HP 36, Speed 30 ft.

STR +1, DEX +1, CON +2, INT +3, WIS +1, CHA +0

Attacks:
- Iron Rod: +4 to hit, 1d6+1 bludgeoning.
- Lantern Pulse (Recharge 5-6): creatures in 15-ft cone make DEX save DC 13 or take 2d6 force and be pushed 5 ft.; half damage/no push on success.

Tactics: destabilize bridge stones, protect the ledger-cleaner, run if the tube is secured.

## Mounted Runner

Medium humanoid mounted on riding horse, AC 14, HP 24, Speed 30 ft. / horse 60 ft.

STR +1, DEX +3, CON +1, INT +0, WIS +1, CHA +0

Attacks:
- Shortsword: +5 to hit, 1d6+3 piercing.
- Light Crossbow: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing.

Tactics: escape with a duplicate route order. Does not fight to death.

## Bridge Hazard

At initiative count 20 each round while unstable, choose one:

1. Stone shifts: one creature on bridge must make DEX save DC 13 or fall prone.
2. Ward flare: random 10-ft line deals 1d6 force, DEX DC 13 half.
3. Cart slips: trapped survivor's danger worsens; after three failures/collapses, survivor takes serious damage.

Use visible rolls for random hazard selection.
