# Protected Character Audit

For every protected character:

- Compare all appearances across logs and documents.
- Extract consistent personality traits.
- Extract recurring speech patterns.
- Extract relationship changes.
- Extract what they know and do not know.
- Identify contradictions.
- Recommend one final canon version.
- Update their protected character file.
- Preserve conflicting older material in source notes or archives.

Prioritize characters who are likely to appear next session or whose voice has drifted in recent play.
