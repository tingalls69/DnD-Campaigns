# DM-Only Secrets and Reveal Discipline

Do not reveal this file to the player as exposition. Use it to structure earned discoveries.

## Core secret

The road-ward corruption and witness-erasure networks are one machine. The same road authority, freight exceptions, route closures, and survey ciphers have been moving stolen ward-iron and disappeared people.

## Orvaine Thresh's real crime

Thresh did not begin as pure evil. He began as a repair official who kept roads open when governments failed. He learned that emergency authority could save lives. Then he decided emergency authority should never end.

His moral collapse:

1. choose which road gets repaired first;
2. choose which town waits;
3. choose which witness is inconvenient;
4. choose which transfer can be hidden;
5. choose which people count as acceptable loss;
6. build a system where no one can see the choice being made.

## Thresh's limitation

Thresh understands old Survey hardware and institutional leverage. He does **not** fully understand the oldest road layer. The ancient road-consciousness, if used, should feel like wounded infrastructure: not a god, not a prophecy machine, but an old promise that remembers being broken.

## Reveal ladder

Do not skip steps.

1. Same cipher appears in road and prisoner records.
2. Same courier route carried ward-iron and people.
3. Same office signed closure notices and transfer orders.
4. Same retired authority mark appears on old Survey hardware.
5. Living prisoners were used as route anchors.
6. Thresh's network is modern, secular, institutional, and only partly cultic.
7. The old roads are reacting to both Brannok's brace and Selene's witness-seal.
8. Thresh thinks he is controlling a machine; he is actually waking a wounded promise.

## Do not do these

- Do not have Thresh monologue the full truth early.
- Do not let a captured minion know every layer.
- Do not make the Ashen Choir secretly responsible for everything.
- Do not make Road Works secretly magical at every level.
- Do not reduce everything to ancient destiny.
- Do not make Brannok or Selene chosen ones.

## What each villain tier knows

### Cleanup crew
Knows where to go, what to burn, who pays, maybe one local supervisor.

### Ledger-cleaner / route agent
Knows ciphers, drop points, and which papers matter. Does not know Thresh personally.

### Choir field captain
Knows prisoners are moved through secular routes. Believes the system serves the Saint's mercy. May know a Benefactor exists.

### Massen Vir
Knows Thresh's logistics methods and enough finance to be terrifying. Does not understand the ancient road layer.

### Thresh
Knows the institutional machine, the old Survey stations, and some witness-erasure utility. Does not understand that the deepest road layer can judge him back.

## Human ending rule

The last reveal should not be a lore dump. It should be a choice.

Examples:

- expose Thresh now and lose the chance to save distant prisoners;
- keep the old engine alive under public witness oversight or break it forever;
- let roads become less safe in the short term so they cannot be secretly controlled;
- put every rescued name on record even if it endangers living witnesses.
