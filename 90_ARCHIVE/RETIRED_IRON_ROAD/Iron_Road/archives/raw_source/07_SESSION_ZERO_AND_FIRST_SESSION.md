# Session Zero and First Session

## Pre-flight questions for the player

Ask these before starting:

1. Are you controlling Brannok, Selene, or both in session one
2. Confirm final solo save states: level, HP, resources, coin, major items.
3. Did Brannok finish a long rest after the Stonewake finale
4. Did the Daughters finish the Vellford finale, and who is injured, missing, or changed
5. Which name choices are final Avoid double Voss / double Mara confusion.
6. Do you want the first meeting tense, cooperative, or hostile-but-practical

## Recommended first-session setup

Start at **Mirren's Crossing**.

Do not begin with lore.

Begin with the bridge failing.

### Opening image

Rain has turned the road to black paste. A trade cart hangs with one wheel over the broken edge of the bridge. One horse is screaming. The ward-stones along the parapet are dark except one, which pulses weakly blue, then white, then blue again like it cannot decide what law it still believes in.

A dead courier lies half under a mile marker. His message tube is wedged beneath the cracked stone.

A survivor is trapped under the axle.

Somewhere in the rain, a cleanup crew is coming back.

Brannok reaches the south approach with road authority, a hammer, and the familiar sick feeling of false safety.

Selene reaches the north approach with the Daughters, a rescued witness's route note, and the familiar taste of someone trying to make a person disappear by moving them through the right door.

They see each other across the bridge.

Neither knows enough yet.

Both know this was done on purpose.

Stop there and ask what the player-controlled protagonist does.

## First-session objectives

- Rescue the trapped survivor.
- Stabilize or retreat from the failing bridge.
- Secure the courier tube.
- Prevent the cleanup crew from destroying evidence.
- Let Brannok and Selene form first impressions through action.

## The courier tube contains

Player-facing:

- a route order marked with the CS7 / Road Works routing family;
- a false closure notice for Mirren's Crossing;
- a prisoner-transfer code that matches Selene's evidence;
- one name: `O. THRESH, COMMISSIONER (RET.)`, only if the solo finales have already planted this name.

DM-only:

- if Thresh has not been revealed in either solo finale, use only the cipher and retired commissioner seal. Save the name for later.

## The cleanup crew

Use practical enemies:

- two road-cutters with axes and pry bars;
- one ledger-cleaner with fire oil;
- one wardwright with a cracked blue lantern;
- one mounted runner trying to escape with the second copy.

Combat objective: stop evidence destruction and prevent the runner from escaping.

## First impression prompts

### Brannok's likely read on Selene

- Too polished for mud.
- Too comfortable being stared at.
- Warlock business is unsettling.
- Clearly not pretending about the trapped survivor.
- Her people move like professionals.

### Selene's likely read on Brannok

- Looks like the road got angry and grew a beard.
- Direct enough to be refreshing, dangerous enough to be annoying.
- Not a fool.
- Cares before he performs caring.
- His crow has terrible manners.

Do not state these as their thoughts. Use them as DM portrayal cues.
