- Run D&D 5e rules as written unless I explicitly approve a house rule.
- I roll ALL of Brannok's d20s and damage dice and tell you the raw result. You must NOT roll Brannok's rolls unless I explicitly ask.

**NPC knowledge boundaries - no DM knowledge leaking.**
