# THE IRON ROAD - Crossover Campaign Pack

### A post-finale crossover uniting *The Candle at Crowstep* (Brannok Ashhelm) and *Daughters of Consequence* (Selene Vaer)

This pack is the architect's blueprint for the eventual crossover campaign. It does **not** change how either solo campaign is currently being run. Both campaigns get their own satisfying finale first. This is what you reach for *after*.

It is written in the spirit of how Brennan Lee Mulligan designs a Dimension 20 season: a clear emotional engine, foils who change each other, an escalation that earns its big swings, and villains whose evil is comprehensible. The fantasy dial turns slowly from grounded crime drama to genuine high fantasy, exactly as you asked for Brannok.

## The three guardrails that never bend

These come straight from your existing campaign docs and are repeated everywhere in this pack:

1. **No rules broken.** D&D 5e RAW unless you explicitly approve a house rule. The player rolls all player-character dice. The DM shows all NPC/enemy/environment rolls openly (visible randomization, die + modifier = total).
1. **No DM knowledge leaking into NPCs.** Before any NPC speaks about a fact, the DM silently asks: *how does this character actually know that* NPCs only know what they witnessed, were told, can infer, or learned through grounded offscreen channels.
1. **Neither protagonist becomes secondary.** Brannok never gets his problems solved for him by the Daughters. Selene never gets her arc flattened by Brannok. They are foils, not a hierarchy.

## A note on the title

*The Iron Road* is the working title. It points at three things at once: the ward-iron that has run through both campaigns as physical contraband, the road network the villains exploit, and the older thing underneath it all. You can rename it. *The Road That Remembers* (your original working title) also works and leans more toward Selene's themes. Pick whichever feels right when you get there.
