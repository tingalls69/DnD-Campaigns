## QUICK REFERENCE - THE WHOLE SEASON IN ONE PARAGRAPH

Two heroes fighting the same forty-year corruption from opposite ends - Brannok the money-and-roads symptom, Selene the people-and-erasure symptom - meet over a dead courier at Mirren's Crossing (Act One, low fantasy), follow a shared cipher to Survey Station Seven where the genre grows teeth and the ground tilts toward something ancient (Act Two, rising fantasy), and converge on the capital where retired Commissioner Orvaine Thresh tries to bury everything under procedure - defeated in a simultaneous courtroom-and-dungeon finale where an old, wounded road-network finally helps the warden who spent his life caring for it (Act Three, high fantasy). No chosen ones. Just two people who refused to stop, and an ending that costs something either way.
