# Canon Locked Facts

Only permanent facts belong here. Do not add guesses, future plans, or vibes.

## Campaign Premise

The Iron Road is a future crossover campaign connecting Brannok's road-ward/CS7 plot and Selene's witness/name/Vellford plot.

## Crossover Gate

The Iron Road must not begin until both solo finales are finished and final save states are documented.

## Player Character Agency

The player controls Brannok and Selene whenever they are player characters. Never decide their actions, thoughts, dialogue, feelings, movement, rolls, spell choices, romantic conclusions, or personal revelations.

## Core Shared Truth

The road-ward corruption and witness-erasure networks are connected through a larger machine of route authority, paperwork, infrastructure, and human predation.

## Main Villain

Orvaine Thresh, Commissioner Retired, is an institutional villain who uses logistics, road authority, closure notices, transfer orders, and old Survey infrastructure to decide who can move, who vanishes, and who counts.

## Brannok Inheritance

Brannok inherits CS7 / Control Node Seven / old Continental Survey / road-ward infrastructure threads.

## Selene Inheritance

Selene inherits Vellford / false names / witness erasure / transfer route / Road Works Survey seal threads.
