# World And Faction Bible

## Core Thesis

A road is a promise: leave here, arrive there, survive the between.

Thresh's crime is turning that promise into controlled access: who moves, who waits, who vanishes, and who counts.

## Locations

### Mirren's Crossing

Opening ward-bridge. Dead courier, failing ward-stones, trapped survivor, half-hanging cart, message tube under cracked marker stone.

### Survey Station Seven

Reclassified old road station four to five days beyond Stonewake's old network. Act Two destination.

### Road Works Archive

Potential Act One evidence/fire scene.

## Factions And Groups

### Thresh Network

Legal paperwork, physical infrastructure, and human predation tied together.

### Road Works / Old Survey Authority

Institutional road control, closure notices, freight exceptions, survey authority, custody overrides.

### Ashen Choir / False Care Networks

Not secretly responsible for everything. They are one human predation layer moved through secular logistics.

## World Rules

- One weird magical concept per scene.
- Road magic should remain physical and playable: ward-stones, bridges, carts, papers, prisoners, mile markers.
- The old road layer is not destiny. It is wounded infrastructure / an old promise that remembers being broken.
