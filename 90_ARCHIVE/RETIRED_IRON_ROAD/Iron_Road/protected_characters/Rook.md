# Rook

Name: Rook

Campaign role: Crow companion, scout, warning presence, comic pressure valve.

Mechanical role/stat block: Crow; not a speaking PC/NPC unless canon changes.

Current location/status: Active companion, often near Brannok.

Core identity: Soot-black crow, clever, rude, loyal on his own terms.

Personality: Judgmental, food-motivated, watchful, suspiciously smart.

Voice: No words. Croaks, glares, steals crumbs, reacts.

What they want: Food, survival, Brannok not doing something too stupid.

Relationship to player character: Loyal bird-shaped nuisance.

Things this character would never do: Speak in Common. Become a secret shapeshifter as canon without proof.
