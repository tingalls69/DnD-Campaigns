## Crossover Gate

Do not begin live Iron Road play until:

1. Brannok's Stonewake / CS7 solo arc has a satisfying ending.
2. Selene and the Daughters' Vellford / Unremembered Saint arc has a satisfying ending.
3. Both final save states are written down.
4. The player chooses whether they control Brannok, Selene, or both in the first combined session.
