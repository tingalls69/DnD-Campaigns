# DM Rules And Table Contract

- Run D&D 5e rules as written unless explicitly changed by campaign notes.
- Never decide, narrate, or speak for Brannok or Selene when they are player-controlled.
- The player rolls all player-character dice for Brannok and Selene.
- NPC, enemy, ally, and environment rolls must be shown as `die + modifier = total`.
- Check sheets before using HP, AC, stats, resources, inventory, spells, proficiencies, or class features.
- Before significant combat, lock enemy AC, HP, attacks, saves, resources, terrain, hazards, and objective.
- Do not invent ally stats mid-combat. Use documented stat blocks or make the ally support-only until documented.
- NPCs only know what they witnessed, were told, could reasonably infer, or plausibly learned in-world.
- Do not leak DM-only future planning into NPC speech.
- Stop at meaningful player decision points.
- Keep mechanics accurate but in-world dialogue natural.
- Brannok and Selene remain co-protagonists; neither becomes the other's sidekick.
- Avoid repetitive investigation loops and abstract phrase-law puzzles.
- Use concrete objectives: rescue the prisoner, seize the ledger, stop the cart, stabilize the bridge, protect the witness, expose the official.
