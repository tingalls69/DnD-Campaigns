## Level 10 Patron Feature: Witness That Cannot Be Erased

Selene has advantage on saving throws against magical effects that would charm her, read her thoughts without permission, alter/erase/fabricate memories, possess her, compel her to answer to a false name or imposed identity, or bind/steal/rewrite/verify/suppress her true name/selfhood.

Once per long rest, when Selene succeeds on one of these saves, she may force the source to make a Wisdom saving throw against DC 17. On a failed save, Selene learns one actionable truth about the source: name, face, location, master, method, or thread connecting it to Selene, chosen by the DM based on the effect.

Flavor trigger: the closed-eye charm goes cold and the words settle: What is witnessed cannot be unmade without consequence.
