# Black Rose Enforcer

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 15
- HP 45
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 16 (+3), DEX 12 (+1), CON 14 (+2), INT 10 (+0), WIS 11 (+0), CHA 10 (+0)

## Attacks
### Mace
Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit. Hit: 1d6+3 bludgeoning.

### Heavy Crossbow
Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit. Hit: 1d10+1 piercing.

## Features
### Door-Breaker
Advantage on Strength checks to force doors, drag prisoners, or hold a line.

### Hired Cruelty
If ordered by a Handler or Auctioneer, the Enforcer gains +1d4 on the next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

## Role
Physical threat, prisoner mover, bodyguard.
