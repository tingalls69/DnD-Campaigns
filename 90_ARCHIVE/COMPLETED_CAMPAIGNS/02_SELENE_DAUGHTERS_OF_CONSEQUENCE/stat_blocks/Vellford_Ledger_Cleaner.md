# Vellford Ledger-Cleaner

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 13
- HP 27
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 9 (-1), DEX 14 (+2), CON 11 (+0), INT 16 (+3), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 12 (+1)

## Skills
Deception +4, Investigation +6, Sleight of Hand +5

## Attacks
### Dagger
+5 to hit. Hit: 1d4+2 piercing.

## Features
### Burn the Trail
As an action, the Cleaner attempts to destroy or alter one document, seal, or ledger entry within reach.

### False Receipt
Once per scene, the Cleaner can impose disadvantage on a check to identify a forged mundane document unless the examiner has specialist tools, prior context, or magic.

## Role
Evidence threat. Capture is more useful than killing.
