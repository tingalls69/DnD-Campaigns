# Black Rose Auctioneer

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 12
- HP 33
- Speed 30 ft.

## Ability Scores
STR 8 (-1), DEX 12 (+1), CON 12 (+1), INT 15 (+2), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 17 (+3)

## Skills
Deception +6, Insight +4, Persuasion +6, Performance +6

## Attacks
### Cane Sword
+4 to hit. Hit: 1d6+1 piercing.

## Features
### Sold Before You Entered
One creature within 30 ft. that can hear the Auctioneer must make WIS save DC 14 or cannot take reactions until the start of its next turn.

### Lot Number
As a bonus action, marks one prisoner, witness, or document as priority. Allied enforcers gain +1d4 on checks to move, seize, or protect that target until the Auctioneer's next turn.

## Role
Social villain, morale pressure, objective manipulator.
