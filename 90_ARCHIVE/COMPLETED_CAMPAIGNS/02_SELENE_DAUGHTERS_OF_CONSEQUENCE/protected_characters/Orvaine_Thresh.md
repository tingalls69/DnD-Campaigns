# Orvaine Thresh

Name: Orvaine Thresh, Commissioner Retired

Campaign role: Main villain of The Iron Road.

Mechanical role/stat block: Missing. Do not run final confrontation until stat block is created.

Current location/status: Offscreen before the crossover begins.

Core identity: An old institutional operator who discovered that logistics is a kind of violence nobody notices until the bodies are already cold.

Personality: Controlled, certain, procedural, paternal in the worst way. He believes emergency authority saved lives once and therefore should never truly end.

Voice: Calm institutional certainty. Not a cult prophet. Not theatrical unless cornered. Speaks in roads, priorities, emergency authority, acceptable loss, and managed movement.

What they want: A continent where movement is managed by people like him: safe for useful commerce, dangerous for inconvenient witnesses, closed for poor towns, open for powerful patrons.

What they fear: Public proof joined to physical evidence; witnesses and records agreeing; the old road layer judging him back.

What they believe: Roads are power because someone decides where they go.

What they know: Institutional machine, old Survey stations, logistics, closure notices, transfer orders, some witness-erasure utility.

What they do not know: The deepest old-road layer as well as he thinks. He does not fully understand what he is waking.

Things this character would never do: Monologue the entire truth early; act like a cackling villain; speak as a cult zealot.

Example dialogue: "You think roads are freedom because you walk them. Roads are power because someone decides where they go."

Common mistakes to avoid: Spooky mastermind, ancient destiny villain, omniscient puppetmaster, Ashen Choir boss.

Source notes: Current Iron Road human-DM pack.
