# Daughters of Consequence - Vellford Finale End State

This document is the clean end-state after the Selene / Daughters of Consequence finale.

## Finale Outcome

Major victory. Selene and the Daughters broke the Vellford Domestic Benevolence / Black Rose false-name network open in public. The Old Rose Conservatory apparatus was destroyed. The three living anchors - Tessa Rill, Annet Crowle, and Merrit Vane - survived and spoke their names. Cressa Durn, known publicly as Cressida Rosevein, was defeated, named, exposed, and taken into custody.

The finale ended with the Daughters alive, Saint Orveth's full of survivors, the recovered ledger secured, and Selene surrounded by her chosen family.

## Essential Names

- Selene Vaer: Protector of Names, Warlock 10, player character.
- Cressa Durn / Cressida Rosevein: final villain, in custody.
- Tessa Rill: red-haired laundry worker, erased as wage-book fraud, rescued.
- Annet Crowle: Ashcombe House housekeeper, key-holder, rescued.
- Merrit Vane: teenage boy anchor-prisoner, rescued and reunited with mother.
- Lysa Vane: Merrit's mother, rescued/stabilized/reunited.
- Nessa Vale / Needle: named by Selene as a Daughter; her name is now protected in Jasna's record.

## Emotional End State

Selene chose not to execute Cressa after surrender. Instead, she named the cycle of abuse, forced truth into public witness, and chose prevention/consequence over revenge. This is a central canon outcome.

The Daughters of Consequence are now a family in truth, not just a name. The final image is not the shattered Conservatory but the Daughters at Saint Orveth's under lantern light, injured, exhausted, alive, surrounded by survivors, and making room for morning.

## Future Use

This finale should be treated as emotionally complete. Future stories can address institutional fallout, survivor recovery, and the broader road-use / Iron Road logistics thread, but should not reopen the Vellford finale as if it were unresolved.
