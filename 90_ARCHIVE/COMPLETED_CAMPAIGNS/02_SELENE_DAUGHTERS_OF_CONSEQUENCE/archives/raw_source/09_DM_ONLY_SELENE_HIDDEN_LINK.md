## Selene / Odessa / Ranna / Marrowmere Link
The hidden link should connect Selene's old survival, Odessa Lark's murder, and the current buyer/altered-subject network without overcomplicating the story.

Core idea:
- Aurelian's hunters killed Odessa because she briefly helped the fleeing girl who would become Selene.
- Ranna Wren witnessed the chase and lied to the hunters, buying Selene time.
- Years later, Marrowmere/Corven/Pike's network held Ranna as a witness asset because she could connect old Tavaril/Aurelian crimes to living testimony.
- Selene may have been wanted not only as a runaway/bloodline problem but also as a potential altered subject, witness-seal carrier, or uniquely valuable name/bloodline specimen.
- Keep this connection direct and emotional, not abstract. The villains wanted people, witnesses, names, and usable bodies. Selene surviving disrupted someone's old plan.

Do not make Ranna instantly recognize adult Selene by face. She reacts to the name, charm, context, and Selene's emotional reaction.

Do not make Odessa into a long-term planner. Odessa met Selene briefly, opened a door, tried to help, and was killed minutes later. That is enough.

Potential future clue:
