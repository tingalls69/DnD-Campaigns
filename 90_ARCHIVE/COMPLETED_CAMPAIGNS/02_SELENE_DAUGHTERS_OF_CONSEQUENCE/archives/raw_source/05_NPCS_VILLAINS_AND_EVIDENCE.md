Former washerwoman/laundry runner near Odessa Lark's home. Saw Selene flee as a girl, saw Odessa help, then lied to Aurelian's hunters and wasted their time. Huge woodsman from Grey Lantern Road. Not altered. Pike muzzled and chained him after Bren refused to track fugitives and helped at least one girl escape using the wrong trail. He knows Grey Lantern trails and can help map the route after food, healing, sleep, and consent. He should be treated away from frightened children until cleaned/rested because he looks terrifying through no fault of his own.

Marrowmere steward captured alive at the hunting lodge. Operated private holding, household servant trafficking, and cleanup. Tied to the green-latched chest and hidden prisoner cupboard.
