## Clothing style notes
- Keep clothing **simple** now: solid colors, small accents, clean lines, light lining, minimal ornament.
- Avoid spider-web patterns or excessive filigree.
- Everyone should look distinct enough that a future AI cannot confuse one Daughter for another.
