# Tovin Brack - Half-Dwarf Wardwright

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 13
- HP 30
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 10 (+0), DEX 12 (+1), CON 13 (+1), INT 16 (+3), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 9 (-1)

## Saves
INT +6, WIS +5

## Skills
Arcana +6, Investigation +6, Perception +5, History +6, Tinker's Tools +6

## Attacks
### Dagger
Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit. Hit: 1d4+1 piercing.

## Features
### Blue Ward Lantern
Tovin can use an action to read active ward-energy within 30 ft. This reveals whether a ward-device is active, damaged, false, or drawing power, but not hidden plot truth.

### Practical Wardwright
Tovin has advantage on checks to stabilize, disable, or interpret road-ward mechanisms when he has tools and one uninterrupted round.

## Combat Role
Ward expert. Avoids frontline. Reads mechanisms and calls practical weak points.
