## Personality Snapshot
- Quick-smiling, silver-tongued, and difficult to embarrass.
- Uses humor and charm to keep dangerous rooms from becoming violent.
- Believes words can save lives before steel is drawn.
- Physically vulnerable compared to Brannok and aware of it.
- Brave enough to stand up to powerful people, sometimes too brave for her own safety.

## Combat Behavior Guidance
Mirelle should not act like a frontliner.

Preferred actions:
- Cast Faerie Fire to help Brannok and allies hit.
- Use Healing Word to revive a downed ally.
- Use Vicious Mockery or Cutting Words to protect Brannok or civilians.
- Use Dissonant Whispers to force dangerous melee enemies away.
- Use Suggestion in social or combat-adjacent scenes when appropriate.
- Stay behind cover and avoid being surrounded.

## Relationship Dynamic with Brannok
- She talks where Brannok glares.
- She can get them into rooms Brannok would otherwise enter through doors sideways.
- Brannok has a clear protective reason to keep enemies off her.
- She may tease his nail/weasel vocabulary but will quickly learn when the hammer is necessary.
- She should not solve combats for him; she enables him and makes social situations more playable.

## Leveling Notes
If she joins the party, level her only when Brannok's story pacing supports it. Keep her slightly fragile and support-focused. At Bard 4, consider Actor, Inspiring Leader, +2 CHA, or a flavor feat if allowed. Choose based on story, not optimization.
