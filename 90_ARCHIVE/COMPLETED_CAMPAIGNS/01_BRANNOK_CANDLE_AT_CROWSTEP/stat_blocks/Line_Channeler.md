# Line Channeler - Altered Ward Conduit

Medium humanoid, altered

## Defense
- AC 13
- HP 32
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 9 (-1), DEX 14 (+2), CON 14 (+2), INT 13 (+1), WIS 10 (+0), CHA 8 (-1)

## Saves
CON +5, INT +4

## Skills
Arcana +4, Perception +3

## Attacks
### Shock Touch
Melee Spell Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2d6 lightning damage.

## Features
### Feed the Node
While touching an active node, the Channeler can use an action to empower it. Until the start of the Channeler's next turn, one connected ward-device gains temporary stability or resistance to disruption.

### Severed Feed
If forcibly moved away from the node, the current empowerment collapses and the Channeler takes 1d6 psychic damage.

## Combat Role
Objective enemy. Dangerous because of what he powers, not raw damage.
