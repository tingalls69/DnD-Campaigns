# Node Warden - Control Node Seven Heavy

Medium humanoid, armored road-authority remnant

## Defense
- AC 17
- HP 68
- Speed 25 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 18 (+4), DEX 10 (+0), CON 16 (+3), INT 12 (+1), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 10 (+0)

## Saves
STR +7, CON +6, WIS +4

## Skills
Athletics +7, Intimidation +3, Insight +4

## Attacks
### Heavy Road Hammer
Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d10+4 bludgeoning.

### Multiattack
The Node Warden makes two Heavy Road Hammer attacks.

## Features
### Road-Plate Armor
The Warden has advantage on Strength checks and saves to resist being shoved or knocked prone while standing on worked road-stone or node flooring.

### Dead Authority
Once per combat when reduced below half HP, the Warden may force one creature within 10 ft. to make a Wisdom save DC 13 or have disadvantage on its next attack before the end of its next turn.

## Combat Role
Mini-boss bruiser. Direct, durable, old authority made violent.
