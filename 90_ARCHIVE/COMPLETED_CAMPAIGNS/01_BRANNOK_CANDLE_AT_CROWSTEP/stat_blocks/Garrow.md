# Garrow - Old Road-Binder

Medium humanoid

## Defense
- AC 12
- HP 36
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 11 (+0), DEX 12 (+1), CON 13 (+1), INT 15 (+2), WIS 16 (+3), CHA 8 (-1)

## Saves
INT +5, WIS +6

## Skills
Arcana +5, History +5, Insight +6, Investigation +5, Survival +6

## Attacks
### Staff
Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit. Hit: 1d6 bludgeoning.

## Features
### Bell-Lock Knowledge
Garrow has advantage on checks involving bell-locks, chain-hearts, old road-binder mechanisms, and obsolete road infrastructure.

### Compromised Helper
Garrow can identify a weak point once per scene if he has seen the mechanism clearly. This should give the party a practical option, not solve the scene alone.

## Combat Role
Dangerous expert under supervision. Utility, not hero.
