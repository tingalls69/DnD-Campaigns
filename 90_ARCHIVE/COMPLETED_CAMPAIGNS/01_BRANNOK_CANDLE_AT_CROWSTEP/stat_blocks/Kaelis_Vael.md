# Kaelis Vael - Route Specialist Archer

Medium humanoid, half-elf

## Defense
- AC 15
- HP 38
- Speed 30 ft.
- Proficiency Bonus +3

## Ability Scores
STR 10 (+0), DEX 16 (+3), CON 12 (+1), INT 14 (+2), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 11 (+0)

## Saves
DEX +6, WIS +5

## Skills
Investigation +5, Perception +5, Stealth +6, Survival +5, Insight +5

## Attacks
### Shortbow
Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 80/320, one target. Hit: 1d6+3 piercing.

### Dagger
Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60. Hit: 1d4+3 piercing.

## Features
### Route Reader
Kaelis has advantage on checks to identify false route markings, altered correction notices, or suspicious road paperwork.

### Covering Shot
Once per round, if Kaelis hits a creature threatening a civilian or restrained ally, that target has disadvantage on its next opportunity attack before Kaelis's next turn.

## Combat Role
Backline overwatch, civilian protection, precision pressure.
