# Running Log - The Candle at Crowstep / Brannok Ashhelm

This log is chronological and should be updated after each major scene. It is separate from the core source document to keep the rules and state readable.

## Character Start
Brannok Ashhelm entered Crowstep as a Mountain Dwarf Fighter with Rook, a soot-black crow. He is blunt, dryly funny, practical, protective, and not dumb. His wisdom comes from lived experience: mines, roads, danger, power, bad work, and consequences.

## Arrival and Public Ritual Failure
Brannok arrived during Crowstep's public candle/basin road-safety ritual. The ritual failed and injured people. Brannok acted immediately, protecting civilians and helping contain the danger. The situation tied to road wards, altered records, and a suspicious ledger/book.

## Ilyra Moss and Early Evidence
Brannok helped protect evidence and people connected to the failed road-safety ritual. He became involved with Crowstep watch and quartermaster matters because he kept finding practical danger behind polished explanations.

## Road-Ward Mystery Clarified
The mystery was corrected away from abstract debt magic. The practical truth: a weekly road-ward safety check failed because a key component chain was sabotaged, hidden, or corrupted. Records were altered so Mara Vell, the quartermaster who should have caught the problem, was removed from the chain.

## Keth Lorram Context
Keth Lorram was not Brannok's long-term friend. They barely knew one another, having met around a road/caravan attack. Keth survived the immediate attack but hid an injury and died before reaching Crowstep. Brannok carried Keth's unopened letter because he is the kind of bastard who finishes the job.

## Marrow / Control-Room Crisis
Brannok, Calder, Nessa, Perrin, and allies confronted the underground control-room problem tied to Marrow and Third Bell. Marrow attempted to redirect the worst road-ward failure onto Saint Orra's west approach to preserve other routes. Brannok helped stop Marrow and stabilize the control room temporarily. Voss and Marrow were captured alive.

## Level to Fighter 3 Champion
After the control-room crisis, Brannok completed a rest and became Fighter 3 Champion. Improved Critical active: weapon attacks crit on natural 19 or 20.

## Crowstep Aftermath and Crest Ledger Shift
Brannok began downtime in Crowstep but quickly uncovered Crest Ledger's profiteering. He followed a hooded man from the Copper Ladle to a map room, confronted Vey Corl and Hasker Noll, and learned about illegal toll control at Old Quarry Cut.

## Old Quarry Cut Ambushes
Brannok went with Calder and watch support to Old Quarry Cut. He broke a red-cap/goblin toll ambush, captured or neutralized enemies, found evidence, and interrogated goblins in Goblin. He learned of a second fake-cart ambush farther along the cut. Brannok and allies broke that ambush too, prevented the horn alarm, captured goblins/red-caps alive where possible, and recovered a Crest-marked strongbox.

## Vey, Hasker, and Crest Proof
The strongbox proved Crest-backed wagons were being prioritized and independents delayed/robbed. Brannok chased down Vey when Vey tried to flee. Vey and Hasker were captured alive, and a contract book implicated Crest Ledger.

## Crest Office and Granary Yard
Brannok helped seize the Crest office before records could be burned. Orven Crest was captured. Evidence pointed to Magdan Crest at the north granary yard. Brannok, Calder, Mara, and watch support confronted Magdan. Two guards chose violence and were dropped quickly. Magdan surrendered. Hidden wagons revealed road spikes, red caps, shortbows, chains, toll boards, and route ledgers showing planned future road seizures.

## Garron Flint, Darik Venn, and Ward Iron
Brannok visited Garron Flint for gear and learned Crest had tried to buy old ward iron. Garron revealed his former apprentice Darik Venn had sold some. Brannok met Darik at the Bent Nail, talked him into cooperation, and recovered a hidden crate of old ward iron from the quarry storehouse after defeating and binding two hired grabbers. Darik returned to Garron for accountability. Garron repaired Brannok's handaxe and fitted a ward-iron shield boss.

## Red-Haired Clerk and Dry Culvert
The grabbers revealed a red-haired Crest clerk with silver-stitched gloves had hired them. Brannok and Calder checked the dry culvert payment drop, found evidence, then tailed the clerk, Lareth Pell, to a shuttered wagon shed. Brannok stopped evidence from being burned, captured Lareth and an associate, and recovered records pointing toward Stonewake buyers.

## Tovan Reed and Skrit's Goblins
In Crowstep market, Brannok learned Tovan Reed's flour wagon had left before closures and not returned. Brannok found Tovan alive near the east road, negotiated with Skrit's goblins in Goblin, paid them two sacks of flour for peace/restitution, recovered Tovan's mule lead and remaining flour, captured a fleeing red-cap alive after a goblin threw a rock at him, and returned Tovan safely to town. He also defended the limping wagon from wolves, killing two and taking them to the Copper Ladle for stew credit.

## Brannok's Backstory Clarified
Brannok left the mines after a preventable collapse caused by corrupt owners using cheap supports, bad iron, and falsified safety records. Before he fully understood the truth, he had been used as muscle to keep workers quiet. He saw signs, waited too long to stand up to those above him, and then dug people out until his hands split. He saved some, but not enough. This drives his hatred of fake safety and people who hide danger behind paperwork.

## Fighter 4 Level-Up
After reviewing XP for Crest, ward-iron proof, Tovan rescue, goblin negotiation, and wolf defense, Brannok advanced to Fighter 4. He chose Crusher with +1 Strength. Strength increased from 19 to 20. Max HP increased to 40 by taking average HP.

## Crowstep Final Prep for Stonewake
Brannok rested at the Copper Ladle, ate, credited an unused room night toward a future stay, and prepared to leave for Stonewake. Calder gave him a Crowstep travel writ, a sealed evidence packet for the Stonewake Ward Office, and a note to Sergeant Elian Voss of the Stonewake Market Guard. Mara gave him a quartermaster seal-strip and 18 gp additional seizure share, warning him that Stonewake sells safety and that clean words can hide danger.

## North Road with Dessa Marn
At Crowstep's north gate, Brannok approached Dessa Marn's wagon and offered muscle for the road. He agreed to travel free if peaceful and charge by the nail if trouble appeared. Dessa, her driver Jory, Brannok, and Rook began north toward Stonewake.

## Marked Cargo Ambush
Brannok noticed a mile marker scratched with three cuts. Rook scouted; Brannok found drag marks and an injured wagon driver hidden off-road. Brannok carried the man to Dessa's wagon while Dessa stabilized him. A hooded crossbowman and a knife-woman cleanup pair were nearby. Brannok defeated the crossbowman, returned toward the wagon, and with Jory/Dessa resisting the knife-woman's attempt to seize Jory, captured both alive.

## North Road Wayhouse
Brannok, Dessa, Jory, the injured driver, and the two bound attackers reached a wayhouse. A healer stabilized the injured driver and explained that three cuts on a mile marker mean marked cargo: a watched wagon, valuable cargo, and likely cleanup crew.

## Old Tollhouse Cargo Recovery
The attackers said the stolen cargo was taken north to the old tollhouse before night. Brannok and Dessa went fast. Brannok approached the tollhouse pretending to be a traveler with stomach trouble, then dropped the act and attacked. He defeated a cudgel man and bowman, intimidated the last inside thief into surrender, and recovered the stolen wagon/cargo. The thief revealed a buyer's agent with a green lantern cart was expected near dark and would ask for "clean wool."

## Green-Lantern Buyer Escape
Brannok chose to return the recovered wagon to the wayhouse rather than wait at the tollhouse. The green-lantern buyer's cart arrived from the north, discovered the failed handoff, and pursued. Dessa drove hard and reached the wayhouse yard with witnesses and guards. The buyer's cart turned off onto a side track and escaped northward, likely toward Stonewake contacts.

## Current Save Point
At the wayhouse, road guards secured the yard and sent riders to collect the three bound tollhouse thieves. The recovered wagon and injured driver are safe. Brannok ate stew, fed Rook bread, and chose to sleep in Dessa's wagon. A long rest has begun. If uninterrupted, he wakes in the morning restored and can continue toward Stonewake Market.
