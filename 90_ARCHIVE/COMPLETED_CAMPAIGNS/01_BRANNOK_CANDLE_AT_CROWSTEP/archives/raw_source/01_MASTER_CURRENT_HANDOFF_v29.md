# 01 Master Current Handoff - The Candle at Crowstep / Brannok Ashhelm

## Short Version
Brannok Ashhelm is a Mountain Dwarf Fighter 5 Champion / Barbarian 2, now in the **final local stretch** of the Stonewake arc. The campaign began as grounded road-ward corruption and has gradually opened into higher fantasy: false ward-gates, road-hungry beasts, ward-binders, bell-locks, old road-iron, and classed NPC allies. The current direction is to finish Stonewake cleanly, then transition to the crossover only after the solo finale lands.

## Brannok in one sentence
A blunt, practical dwarf whose mine-collapse guilt makes him violently intolerant of fake safety, bad paperwork, and polite people hiding danger.

## What he believes
Brannok believes he waited too long once and people died. Since then he refuses to wait when the road, the poor, or the vulnerable are being used. He is not reckless for its own sake; he is protective, direct, and stubborn.

## Important history, condensed
- Crowstep began with road-ward sabotage, false claims, and corrupt "safety" systems.
- Brannok learned to rely on grounded allies: Nera (Carter's Rest), Voss (Ward sergeant), Mirelle (ledger/legal), Kaelis (bow ally), Pellan Orsik (smith), Tovin Brack (wardwright), and Rook (crow companion).
- The Ward Hall hearing exposed the obvious timing issues with Halven, the red ledger, and the question of who had Brannok's route before he arrived.
- The old bridge ward-gate fight revealed false ward-iron, ward-hounds, and a Glimmerjaw. Brannok and the crew pulled a false bracket and temporarily stabilized the gate.
- Red Willow Crossing exposed a second-layer sabotage plan hidden inside medicine crates.
- South Toll Cut revealed a bell-lock / chain-heart mechanism that opened a road breach for a half-breached creature. Brannok broke the lower pin and shut the road.
- Rell Garrow, an old road-binder, was captured/coerced into helping and pointed toward larger road-routing marks beyond Stonewake.
- The current move was the survey store on the east ridge, where the gray-coated route woman and a tarp-covered stitched shape were guarding more ward-iron wedges and route material. They were brought down, exposing the next local cache and pushing the story straight toward the finale.

## Current truth of the arc
The road-control network is not random. It is a real system of old road-ward parts, false repairs, route ciphers, and people who know how to make a road lie. Brannok is now in the final local act, where the remaining job is to expose the last active node, break the chain, and close Stonewake cleanly before any crossover material becomes central.
