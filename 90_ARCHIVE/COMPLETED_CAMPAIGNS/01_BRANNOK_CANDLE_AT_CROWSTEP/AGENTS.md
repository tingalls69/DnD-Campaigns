# Campaign Repository Rules

- Preserve canon. Never delete established campaign information; archive outdated or duplicate material.
- Never flatten the campaign into one giant file.
- Keep active play files short, current, and useful at the table.
- Treat `02_SESSION_PACKET.md` as the most important live-play document.
- Update corrections into permanent files, not only summaries.
- Protected characters must have dedicated files in `protected_characters/`.
- Never convert future plans into current truth.
- After every session update, refresh `02_SESSION_PACKET.md` and `04_CURRENT_CAST.md` first.
