### Ashhelm Road-Hammer, Awakened

- +1 weapon.

Core phrase: "Shield says no. Hammer says I meant it."

#### Wardbreaker Heart

Once per short rest, when Brannok hits a creature, object, or warded structure made from, protected by, or empowered by ward-iron, he deals an extra 1d4 force damage. If the target is false ward-iron, a fake road ward, a corrupted ward-anchor, or a similar false/corrupt road-ward effect, the extra damage becomes 1d6 force damage.

#### Shelter-Breaker, Shelter-Shield

Once per short rest, when Brannok hits a creature that has threatened, harmed, seized, or tried to move past him toward a creature, doorway, shelter, prisoner, child, witness, road-worker, or ward-anchor under his protection within the last minute, the target must make a DC 16 Strength saving throw. On a failure, Brannok chooses one:

- Push the target up to 10 feet.
- Knock it prone.
- Bar its advance: until the start of Brannok's next turn, the target cannot willingly move closer to one protected creature, doorway, shelter entrance, or ward-anchor Brannok names.

#### Resonant Warning

When Shelter-Breaker, Shelter-Shield could apply, Brannok feels the hammer answer with a low resonance. This does not identify hidden enemies, force action, override Brannok's judgment, or act as automatic threat detection.

Guardrail from Hedra: if the hammer starts answering when there is nothing to protect, stop using it and find her.

## Roleplay Core

Brannok is blunt, practical, morally serious, and not stupid. He is suspicious of polished authority and fake safety. His rage is not mindless violence; it is disciplined protection. He does not seek destiny; he looks for the next people in danger.
