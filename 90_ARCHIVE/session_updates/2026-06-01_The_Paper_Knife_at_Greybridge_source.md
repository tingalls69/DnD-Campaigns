# Source Session Update - The Paper Knife At Greybridge

This archive note preserves the player-provided end-of-session update used to refresh active Last Lantern files on 2026-06-01.

## Session Title

The Paper Knife at Greybridge

## Canon Summary

The party woke in Greybridge after the Hearthline rescue. A false official, Halven Rusk, attempted to move Hearthline witnesses using official-looking transfer paperwork. Mirelle spotted that the seal was real but the destination line was wrong: South Lantern Relief Annex, a decommissioned or false destination.

The transfer targeted Pella Morn, a young Hearthline witness with a burn scar on her wrist. When exposed, the operation turned violent: rooftop knifemen, hook-blade wagon attackers, hostile escorts, and a masked coordinator with a road-arch token attacked.

Brannok and Selene stopped the operation. Ves dropped both rooftop knifemen. Zula suppressed/bound the road-arch tokens. Mara protected Pella and the civilian line. Marcella supported morale and used Dissonant Whispers. Corren fought in the yard and helped stabilize the masked coordinator. Brindle and Greybridge guards secured prisoners.

Pella revealed she knows a hidden servant-road entrance to Bellweather House beneath the rain steps and behind the old ash trough, accessible when water is running. She has heard the south bell once in her sleep. A recovered note says: "Take the Morn girl alive. Paper first. Knife second. Bellweather if exposed. Do not let her hear the south bell twice."

The session ended after a short rest inside the Greybridge wayhouse. Prisoners are secured. Pella is safe for now. The party's next likely action is planning Bellweather House / South Lantern Relay.
