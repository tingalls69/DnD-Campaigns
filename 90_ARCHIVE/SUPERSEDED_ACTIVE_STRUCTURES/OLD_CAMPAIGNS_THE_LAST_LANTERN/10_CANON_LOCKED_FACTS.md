# 10 Canon Locked Facts - The Last Lantern / Iron Road

## Player Agency

Brannok and Selene are player-controlled. Never speak, roll, decide, move, or emotionally conclude for them.

## Rook

Rook is a soot-black crow. He cannot speak words. He may caw, croak, gesture, steal food, scout, dance, warn, or react.

## Ves

Ves is a cat-sized pseudodragon with catlike personality. She is not a cat. She cannot speak words. She is proud, possessive, dramatic, loyal to Selene, and emotionally important.

## Brannok Core Wound / End-State

Brannok saw signs in the mines, waited too long, and people died. He will not wait again. His solo finale reinforced his refusal of acceptable-loss logic and his commitment to ordinary people on dangerous roads.

## Brannok Stonewake / Carter's Rest Finale Canon

- Brannok is Fighter 6 Champion / Barbarian 2, Level 8, XP 37,900 / 48,000, HP 92/92.
- Carter's Rest survived Rell Vant's attack.
- The children, witnesses, ledger, and key allies survived.
- Rell Vant was defeated nonlethally and captured alive.
- Field Box Three revealed `O.T.` and `IRON ROAD / LAST LANTERN HOLD UNTIL CALLED`.
- Brannok's Ashhelm Road-Hammer is awakened.
- Brannok's Carter's Rest Shield is refit and carries part of the inn's wall with him.

## Selene Core Wound / End-State

People tried to erase, own, rename, and use Selene. She refuses to be unmade and fights for witnesses to matter. Her Vellford finale is complete: she destroyed the Old Rose Conservatory apparatus, saved living anchors, exposed Cressa Durn, and chose consequence/prevention over revenge.

- Old Rose Conservatory apparatus was destroyed.

- Merrit Vane reunited with Lysa Vane.

- Nessa Vale / Needle was named as a Daughter.
- Ves survived a near-death heroic moment.
- Selene's title: **Protector of Names**.
- Strict XP after finale: Selene/Kara/Jasna/Zula/Nessa 72,750 / 85,000; Eya/Marcella 64,500 / 85,000; Mara 48,000 / 64,000.

## Villain Spine

The enemy believes some lives must be sacrificed, routed, erased, or renamed for the system to function. This is the shared moral enemy.

## Evidence Rule

Evidence matters, but should lead to action scenes, rescue targets, public confrontations, or hard choices. It should not become the whole campaign loop.

## Crossover Protection

Do not undo either solo finale. The crossover threat is a larger institutional/logistics system, not a reveal that Brannok or Selene secretly failed.
