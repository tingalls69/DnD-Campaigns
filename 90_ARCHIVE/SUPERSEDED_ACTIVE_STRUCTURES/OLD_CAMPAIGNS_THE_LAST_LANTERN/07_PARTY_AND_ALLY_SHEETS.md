# 07 Party and Ally Sheets

## Principle
Ally sheets exist so the DM does not invent numbers mid-scene. Allies should help, complicate, protect, banter, and reveal values. They must not solve the campaign for Brannok or Selene.

## Combat Tiering
### Full Spotlight Allies
Use only a few per combat as full initiative actors.
Possible choices: Kara, Mara, Zula, Needle, Marcella, Mirelle, Kaelis, Voss, Pellan, Tovin.

### Support Wing Allies
All others act as wings with simple objectives:
- hold civilians;
- evacuate wagons;
- preserve records;
- stabilize wards;
- block exits;
- protect children;
- counter a secondary threat.

## Rook and Ves
Rook and Ves are emotionally important but should not become tactical problem-solvers unless the player directs or the scene specifically supports it.

## Required Prep
Before combat, decide who is full initiative and who is support wing. Record it in the encounter lock sheet.
