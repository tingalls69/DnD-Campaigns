# 03 Current State - The Last Lantern

## Brannok Side

Brannok has completed the Stonewake / Carter's Rest finale and exposed CS7 as larger than Stonewake. He is Fighter 6 Champion / Barbarian 2, Level 8, XP 37,900 / 48,000, HP 92/92, carrying the emotional weight of Control Node Seven, Carter's Rest, and the truth that someone is still routing harm through ordinary roads.

Core active truths:

- CS7 is an old Continental Survey / road-authority mark.
- O. Thresh / Orvaine Thresh is tied to iron routing and old institutional control.
- Stonewake was a waypoint, not the end.
- Carter's Rest survived Rell Vant's attack; the children, witnesses, ledger, and key allies survived.
- Rell Vant was captured alive.
- Field Box Three points toward `IRON ROAD / LAST LANTERN HOLD UNTIL CALLED`.
- Brannok hates acceptable-loss logic more than almost anything.
- Rook is a soot-black crow who cannot speak words but can absolutely steal bread and become beloved by children.

## Selene Side

Selene's Daughters of Consequence solo finale is complete. She and the Daughters broke the Vellford Domestic Benevolence / Black Rose false-name network, destroyed the Old Rose Conservatory apparatus, saved the living anchors, recovered the ledger, and exposed Cressa Durn / Cressida Rosevein publicly.

Core completed truths:

- Selene is Warlock 10, strict XP 72,750 / 85,000, publicly/emotionally titled **Protector of Names**.
- The Daughters are a true family: Kara, Eya, Jasna, Zula, Nessa/Needle, Marcella, Mara, and Ves all survived.
- Rescued/named victims include Tessa Rill, Annet Crowle, Merrit Vane, and Lysa Vane.
- Cressa Durn is in custody, exposed as both survivor of erasure and perpetrator of erasure.
- Nessa Vale / Needle was named as a Daughter and protected in Jasna's record.
- Ves survived a near-death heroic moment and remains emotionally central.
- The remaining road-use / logistics mark is a future institutional thread, not proof that Vellford was unresolved.

## Crossover State

Both solo arcs now have emotionally complete endings available as bridge points. The crossover may begin only after the user is ready, and should begin through a living crisis: refugees, children, a road shelter, a last lantern still burning, and Brannok/Selene arriving from different directions to save the same people.

## What Not To Do

Do not open with "the evidence points to three roads."
Do not make the first meeting a prophecy or council-room briefing.
Do not bury the player in old survey law.
Do not make either protagonist look foolish to elevate the other.
Do not undo Brannok's Stonewake victory or Selene's Vellford victory.
