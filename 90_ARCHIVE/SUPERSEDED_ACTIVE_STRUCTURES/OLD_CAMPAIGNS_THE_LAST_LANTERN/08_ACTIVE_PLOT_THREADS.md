# 08 Active Plot Threads

## Human-First Threads
1. The Hearthline refugee crossing must survive.
2. Children and witnesses from multiple systems are in the same place for the first time.
3. Brannok and Selene must learn each other through choices, not exposition.
4. The Daughters and Brannok's allies must become a living cast, not a crowd.

## Villain Thread
Orvaine Thresh and the old acceptable-loss network are still routing danger through roads, records, names, and logistics.

## Concrete Threats
- ward failures near populated routes;
- refugee caravan ambushes;
- collaborators hiding among relief workers;
- witnesses targeted before they can speak;
- road devices powered by living anchors;
- nobles/officials trying to sanitize atrocity as necessity;
- monsters entering through damaged ward-gates.

## Retired as Primary Gameplay
- "Evidence points to three roads."
- Long legalistic route-law debates.
- Abstract invitation/name metaphysics as scene engines.

Clues should point to people, danger, locations, rescues, or choices.
