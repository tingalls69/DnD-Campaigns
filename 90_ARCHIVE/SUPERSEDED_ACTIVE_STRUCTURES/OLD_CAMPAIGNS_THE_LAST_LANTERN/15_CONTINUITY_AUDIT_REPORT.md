# 15 Continuity Audit Report

## Audit Result

This pack intentionally overhauls the prior The Last Lantern story spine.

## Preserved

- Brannok and Selene remain equal protagonists.
- CS7 / Thresh / road authority concepts remain usable.
- Road wards remain practical fantasy infrastructure.
- Witness/name/consequence themes remain part of Selene's world.
- Brannok's road-safety and acceptable-loss themes remain central.
- The crossover waits for satisfying solo finales.

## Changed

- Iron Road is now superseded as the active campaign branch by The Last Lantern.
- The campaign no longer centers on documents pointing to route options.
- The first meeting is rebuilt around protecting living people.
- The emotional engine is refuge, downtime, rescue, public courage, and consequence.
- Thresh is framed primarily as a human institutional villain, not an abstract metaphysical force.

## Latest Reconciliation

- Brannok's older Level 7 / Fighter 5 state is archived history. Active current canon is Level 8 / Fighter 6 Champion / Barbarian 2 after the Carter's Rest finale.
- Daughters finale completion is current canon, not future planning.
- Old Iron Road files are preserved as archived planning and should not override The Last Lantern active files.

## Risk Checks

- Do not let Brannok become generic dwarf fighter.
- Do not let Selene become generic charismatic warlock.
- Do not let Marcella become generic girlfriend.
- Do not let Mirelle become generic bard companion.
- Do not let Rook or Ves become talking mascots.
- Do not let the Daughters become a nameless support blob.
