# Hearthline Mass Combat Framework

## Full Combatants
Brannok and Selene always get full spotlight. Choose 2-4 allies for full initiative.

## Support Wings
- Daughters Wing: protects civilians / counters magic / preserves witnesses.
- Ward Crew Wing: stabilizes road devices.
- Hearthline Guard Wing: holds barricades.
- Refugee Wing: moves if protected, panics if neglected.

## Round Structure
1. Resolve enemy spotlight turns.
2. Resolve selected full ally turns.
3. Resolve support wing checks only if stakes change.
4. Stop at Brannok or Selene turns.

## Objectives Beyond Damage
- keep three wagons moving;
- stop saboteur reaching the ward pin;
- prevent civilian casualties;
- preserve one living witness;
- keep the road open for six rounds;
- decide whether to chase the commander or hold the line.
