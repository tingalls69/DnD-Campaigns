# Kaelis Vael - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Survivor, route specialist, bow-capable ally. More than what happened to her.

## Voice Rules
Careful, sharp, recovering agency. Does not perform gratitude on command.

## What They Know
Her correction, captivity, route work, what she witnessed.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her "the rescued one" forever.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
She takes the shot or correction that saves people because she chooses to act.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
