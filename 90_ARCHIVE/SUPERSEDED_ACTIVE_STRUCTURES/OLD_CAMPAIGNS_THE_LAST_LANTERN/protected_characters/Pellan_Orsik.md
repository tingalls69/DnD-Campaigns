# Pellan Orsik - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Dwarf smith, blunt honest craft, road-metal expert.

## Voice Rules
Gravel, craft metaphors, no nonsense.

## What They Know
Metal, old road components, what Brannok showed him.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make him magical blacksmith vending machine.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
He reforges or repairs something symbolic before danger.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
