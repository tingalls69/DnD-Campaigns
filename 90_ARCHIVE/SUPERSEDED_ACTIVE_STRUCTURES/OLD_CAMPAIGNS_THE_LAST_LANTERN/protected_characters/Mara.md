# Mara - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Blunt fighter, guard, bodyguard, the door. Hard exterior, protective heart.

## Voice Rules
Short, direct, unimpressed. Softness appears as action.

## What They Know
Daughters events, guarded people, what she saw. Not subtle lore.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her just muscle or secretly eloquent in speeches.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Door She Chooses: she holds a route while civilians escape.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.

## Post-Vellford Finale Canon

Mara held the Ashcombe House door while prisoners escaped and helped escort Lysa Vane toward Merrit. The finale cemented Mara's identity as "the door" by choice, not just muscle. Mara is now Battle Master Fighter 9, HP 77, STR 18, PB +4, Indomitable 1/long rest, maneuver DC 16.
