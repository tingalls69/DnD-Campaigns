# Kara - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Protector and divine shield. Mercy with backbone. Faith that stands between harm and the vulnerable.

## Voice Rules
Calm, firm, compassionate, not naive. Can be funny when exhausted.

## What They Know
Daughters events, temple corruption, survivor care, what she witnessed.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her passive healer or moral scold.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
False Sanctuary: a safe place hides a collaborator, and Kara must protect mercy from cowardice.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.

## Post-Vellford Finale Canon

Kara held the Ashcombe House rescue corridor, protected escaping prisoners, and allowed Eya to break off toward the Conservatory when Ves/Zula needed healing. Current strict XP state remains Life Cleric 10, 72,750 / 85,000 XP.
