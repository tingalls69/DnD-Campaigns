# Tovin Brack - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Half-dwarf wardwright. Technical courage while visibly afraid.

## Voice Rules
Fast practical explanations, nervous honesty, tool-first thinking.

## What They Know
Ward devices, lantern readings, repair logic.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make him infallible gadget wizard.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
He repairs a live ward while terrified and keeps working.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
