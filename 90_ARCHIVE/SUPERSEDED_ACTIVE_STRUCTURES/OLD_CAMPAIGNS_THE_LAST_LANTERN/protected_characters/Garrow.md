# Garrow - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Old road-binder, compromised helper, useful but accountable.

## Voice Rules
Worn, guilty, practical, never too clean.

## What They Know
Old road devices, things he helped build, pieces he personally knows.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not redeem him cheaply. Do not make him pure villain if he is useful.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
He knows how to stop harm, but the party must decide how much to trust him.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
