# Ves - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Cat-sized pseudo-dragon familiar with catlike personality. Proud, possessive, dramatic, loyal to Selene.

## Voice Rules
Nonverbal hisses, trills, violet-eyed judgment, shoulder perching, offended body language.

## What They Know
What she sees/hears near Selene and familiar-level instincts.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not make her a cat. Do not make her speak. Do not turn her into a combat engine.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
The Small Guardian: a survivor trusts the little dragon before trusting the people.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.

## Post-Vellford Finale Canon

Ves was a major heroine of the Vellford finale. She stung multiple enemies, protected Merrit, dropped an interposing guard with poison, was knocked unconscious by Cressa's Polite Cruelty, and was healed by Zula and Eya. Selene later praised Ves privately and warned her she would be publicly embarrassed forever if she scared Selene like that again.

Ves remains nonverbal, proud, cat-sized, emotionally important, and protected. Do not use Ves as disposable tactical damage.
