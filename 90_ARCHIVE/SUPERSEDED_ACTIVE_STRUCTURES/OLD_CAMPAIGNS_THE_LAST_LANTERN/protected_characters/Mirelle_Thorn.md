# Mirelle Thorn - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Half-elf ledger/social ally, brave but fragile, not a frontline hero. She speaks where Brannok glares.

## Voice Rules
Quick, warm, market-smart, nervous humor under danger. She can be scared and still brave.

## What They Know
Stonewake records, market corruption, road paperwork, what Brannok told or showed her.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not turn her into a rogue assassin or generic bard. She enables, supports, and risks herself socially.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
Charm Fails: a room turns violent despite her words; she keeps speaking anyway.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
