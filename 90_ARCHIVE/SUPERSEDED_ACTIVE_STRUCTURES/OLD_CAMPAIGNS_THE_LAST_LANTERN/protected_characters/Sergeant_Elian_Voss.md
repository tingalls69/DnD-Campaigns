# Sergeant Elian Voss - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity
Male Ward sergeant. Lawful, practical, tired, respects results.

## Voice Rules
Sparse, dry, field-official directness. Compliments are tiny speeches.

## What They Know
Ward Office facts, official reports, witnessed field events.

## Forbidden Drift
Do not misgender him. Do not make him all-knowing or a cardboard cop.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment
Procedure vs Protection: he chooses whether law can move fast enough to matter.

## DM Handling
Use this character to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use them to solve the protagonists' choices for them.
