# Brannok Ashhelm - Protected Character Profile

## Core Identity

Mountain dwarf hammer-and-shield protector. Fighter 6 Champion / Barbarian 2, Level 8. Blunt, morally serious, funny by accident and deflection, not stupid. His mine-collapse guilt is the core wound: he saw signs, waited too long, people died, and he will not wait again.

## Current Mechanical Anchor

- XP 37,900 / 48,000.
- HP 92/92.
- AC 18.
- STR 20, DEX 14, CON 18, INT 12, WIS 16, CHA 8.
- Awakened Ashhelm Road-Hammer: +1 weapon, +8 to hit, 1d8+8 normal, 1d8+10 raging.
- Carter's Rest Shield: Hold Behind Me 1/short rest.
- Rage 2/long rest, Action Surge 1/short rest, Second Wind 1d10+6, Extra Attack, Improved Critical, Crusher.

## Voice Rules

Plain speech. Hammers, nails, rats, weasels, food, work, roads. Humor covers grief. Serious words should be simple and heavy.

## What They Know

Brannok knows what he witnessed in his campaigns, what allies told him, practical road-ward truths, and the Stonewake / Carter's Rest forward evidence from Field Box Three. He does not know Selene's secrets unless she reveals them.

## Current Canon State

Brannok defended Carter's Rest from Archivist Rell Vant and the remaining Stonewake conspirators. The inn, children, witnesses, ledger, and key allies survived. Rell was captured alive. Field Box Three points toward `IRON ROAD / LAST LANTERN HOLD UNTIL CALLED`.

## Forbidden Drift

Do not make him a generic dumb dwarf, murderhobo, or comic relief brute. His violence is protective, not random. Do not have the hammer choose for him. Do not treat his rage as mindless.

## Key Emotional / Important Moment

The Acceptable Loss Argument: someone says a town or group had to be sacrificed. Brannok gets to answer with joke, silence, speech, or hammer.

## DM Handling

Use Brannok to create pressure, humor, tenderness, or consequence. Do not use him to solve Selene's choices or to diminish Selene. Never decide his words, actions, rolls, thoughts, or emotional conclusions.
