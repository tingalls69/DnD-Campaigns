# AGENTS.md

## Repository Agent Rules

When updating this campaign:
1. Preserve player agency for Brannok and Selene.
2. Keep future plans separate from current truth.
3. Do not move DM-only secrets into player-facing files.
4. Maintain append-only session logs.
5. Update protected character files after major character moments.
6. Use the current session packet as the first live-play reference.
7. Keep the human-first campaign priority above older lore fidelity.

## Anti-Drift Reminder
The campaign should feel human-made: specific, funny, emotional, and playable. If a scene becomes a bureaucracy puzzle, convert it into a person to save, a villain to confront, a place to survive, or a choice with consequences.
