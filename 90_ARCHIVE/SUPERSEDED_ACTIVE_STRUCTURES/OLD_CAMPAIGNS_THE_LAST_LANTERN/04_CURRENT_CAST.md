# 04 Current Cast

## Protagonists

### Brannok Ashhelm

Blunt dwarf Fighter 6 Champion / Barbarian 2, Level 8. Hammer and shield. Practical, morally serious, funny in a rough way, not stupid. Carries guilt from a preventable mine collapse where he saw signs and waited too long. His rage is disciplined protective fury. Carter's Rest survived because he trusted allies and held the line.

### Selene Vaer

Charismatic warlock, dramatic, wounded, sharp, and deeply tied to chosen family. Her story is identity, consequence, witnesses, memory, and refusing to be owned or erased. She can be terrifying, hilarious, vulnerable, and kind in the same scene.

## New / Rebuilt Crossover NPCs

### Commander Rhea Varric

Leader of the Hearthline refugee crossing. Ex-soldier turned evacuation commander. Tired, funny when cornered, brutally practical, no patience for heroes making speeches while wagons are stuck.

### Orvaine Thresh

The institutional villain. Not a god. Not a concept. A person who believes civilization requires choosing acceptable losses. Calm, patient, horrifyingly reasonable, and personally responsible for routing harm.

### The Hearthline Children

Not plot devices. A recurring chorus of fear, jokes, bad questions, Rook fandom, Ves worship, and reminders that the road matters because people live on it.
