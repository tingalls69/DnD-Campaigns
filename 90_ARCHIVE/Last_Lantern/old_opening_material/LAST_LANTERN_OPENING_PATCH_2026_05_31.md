# Last Lantern Opening Patch - 2026-05-31

This document is the current override for The Last Lantern opening.

If this document conflicts with archived Iron Road, old Last Lantern, Carter's Rest opening, or finale-to-crossover documents, this document wins.

## Core Revision

The Last Lantern begins three months after the Brannok and Selene finales. It should feel like a new chapter, not the next morning.

## Opening Location

Do not open at Carter's Rest.

Open at the South Lantern Waystation: a temporary fortified refuge built around an old road bell, a cracked ward-post, parked wagons, canvas kitchens, injured witnesses, children, tired guards, and people trying very hard not to panic.

## Active Traveling Roster

Brannok travels with:

- Mirelle Thorn.
- Corren Dask.
- Rook / Central Bird.

Selene travels with:

- Marcella Viretti.
- Mara.
- Needle / Nessa Vale.
- Zula Ozu.
- Ves.

Kara, Eya, and Jasna stay behind as Daughters leadership/support. They are not absent from the world; they are not in the starting travel party.

## Opening Crisis

The road bell rings wrong. Three threats happen at once:

1. Ward-post failure at the north edge of camp.
2. Wagon line collapse at the east wagon line.
3. False relief worker attack at the records wagon.

Stop and ask what Brannok and Selene do. Ask for each protagonist separately if needed. Do not assume they coordinate or prioritize the same threat.

## First Session Target

The player should leave thinking, "These people need us," not, "I need to understand the lore."
